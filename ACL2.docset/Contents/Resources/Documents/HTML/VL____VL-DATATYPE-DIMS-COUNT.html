Definitions and Theorems

Function: vl-datatype-dims-count

(defun vl-datatype-dims-count (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard (vl-datatype-p x)))
       (let ((__function__ 'vl-datatype-dims-count))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (+ (len (vl-datatype->udims x))
               (len (vl-datatype->pdims x)))))
