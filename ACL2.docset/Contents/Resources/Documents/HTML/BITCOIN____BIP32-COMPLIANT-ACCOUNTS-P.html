This is similar to bip32-compliant-addresses-p.

Theorem: bip32-compliant-accounts-p-suff

(defthm
 bip32-compliant-accounts-p-suff
 (implies
  (and
    (natp account-index-limit)
    (bip32-compliant-accounts-for-limit-p tree account-index-limit))
  (bip32-compliant-accounts-p tree)))
