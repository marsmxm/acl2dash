(vl-module-oddexpr-check x ss) carries our our oddexpr-check on all the expressions in a module, and adds any resulting warnings to the module.

Function: vl-module-oddexpr-check

(defun
 vl-module-oddexpr-check (x ss)
 (declare (xargs :guard (and (vl-module-p x)
                             (vl-scopestack-p ss))))
 (let
  ((__function__ 'vl-module-oddexpr-check))
  (declare (ignorable __function__))
  (b*
    ((x (vl-module-fix x))
     ((when (vl-module->hands-offp x)) x)
     (ss (vl-scopestack-push (vl-module-fix x)
                             ss))
     (ctxexprs (vl-module-ctxexprs x))
     (new-warnings (vl-exprctxalist-oddexpr-check ctxexprs ss)))
    (change-vl-module x
                      :warnings (append new-warnings
                                        (vl-module->warnings x))))))
