Floor of an N-bit signed by an integer is usually an N-bit signed. (Weak form, enabled by default).

See also basic-signed-byte-p-of-floor-split.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: basic-signed-byte-p-of-floor

(defthm basic-signed-byte-p-of-floor
        (implies (and (signed-byte-p n a)
                      (integerp b)
                      (not (and (equal a (- (expt 2 (- n 1))))
                                (equal b -1))))
                 (signed-byte-p n (floor a b))))
