Modifying constructor for vl-interfaceport structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-interfaceport x 
                         [:name <name>] 
                         [:ifname <ifname>] 
                         [:loc <loc>] 
                         [:modport <modport>] 
                         [:udims <udims>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-interfaceport.

We construct a new vl-interfaceport structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-interfaceport

(defmacro
     change-vl-interfaceport (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'vl-interfaceport
                            x args
                            '((:name . vl-interfaceport->name)
                              (:ifname . vl-interfaceport->ifname)
                              (:loc . vl-interfaceport->loc)
                              (:modport . vl-interfaceport->modport)
                              (:udims . vl-interfaceport->udims))
                            'change-vl-interfaceport
                            'remake-vl-interfaceport))
