Recognize expression terms returning booleans.

Signature
(atc-boolean-termp term) → yes/no
Arguments
term — Guard (pseudo-termp term).
Returns
yes/no — Type (booleanp yes/no).

We just check whether the term is a call of a boolean-from-<type> function or a call of not, and, or or.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: atc-boolean-termp

(defun
 atc-boolean-termp (term)
 (declare (xargs :guard (pseudo-termp term)))
 (let
  ((__function__ 'atc-boolean-termp))
  (declare (ignorable __function__))
  (b*
     (((mv andp & &) (check-and-call term))
      ((when andp) t)
      ((mv orp & &) (check-or-call term))
      ((when orp) t))
     (case-match term
                 ((fn . &)
                  (if (or (member-eq fn *atc-boolean-from-type-fns*)
                          (eq fn 'not))
                      t nil))
                 (& nil)))))
