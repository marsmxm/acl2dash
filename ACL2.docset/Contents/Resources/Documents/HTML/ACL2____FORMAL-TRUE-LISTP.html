The definition of true-listp on formal terms.

Note that FORMAL-TRUE-LISTP is a `formal' predicate returning (QUOTE T) or (QUOTE NIL).

Function: formal-true-listp

(defun formal-true-listp (term)
       (declare (xargs :guard (pseudo-termp term)))
       (case-match term
                   (('quote x)
                    (cons 'quote
                          (cons (true-listp x) 'nil)))
                   (('cons x y)
                    (declare (ignore x))
                    (formal-true-listp y))
                   (& *nil*)))
