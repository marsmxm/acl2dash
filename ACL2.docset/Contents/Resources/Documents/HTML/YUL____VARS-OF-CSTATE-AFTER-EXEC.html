Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: keys-of-cstate->local-of-exec-statement-list

(defthm
 keys-of-cstate->local-of-exec-statement-list
 (b*
  ((outcome (exec-statement-list stmts cstate funenv limit)))
  (implies
    (not (resulterrp outcome))
    (subset
         (omap::keys (cstate->local cstate))
         (omap::keys (cstate->local (soutcome->cstate outcome)))))))
