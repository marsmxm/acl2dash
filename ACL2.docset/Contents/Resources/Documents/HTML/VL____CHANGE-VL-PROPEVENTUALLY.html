Modifying constructor for vl-propeventually structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-propeventually x 
                          [:strongp <strongp>] 
                          [:range <range>] 
                          [:prop <prop>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-propeventually.

We construct a new vl-propeventually structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-propeventually

(defmacro
    change-vl-propeventually (x &rest args)
    (std::change-aggregate 'vl-propeventually
                           x args
                           '((:strongp . vl-propeventually->strongp)
                             (:range . vl-propeventually->range)
                             (:prop . vl-propeventually->prop))
                           'change-vl-propeventually
                           'nil))
