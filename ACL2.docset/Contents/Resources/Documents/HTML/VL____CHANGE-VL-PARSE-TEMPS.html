Modifying constructor for vl-parse-temps structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-parse-temps x 
                       [:ansi-p <ansi-p>] 
                       [:ansi-ports <ansi-ports>] 
                       [:paramports <paramports>] 
                       [:imports <imports>] 
                       [:loaditems <loaditems>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-parse-temps.

We construct a new vl-parse-temps structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-parse-temps

(defmacro
 change-vl-parse-temps (x &rest args)
 (std::change-aggregate 'vl-parse-temps
                        x args
                        '((:ansi-p . vl-parse-temps->ansi-p)
                          (:ansi-ports . vl-parse-temps->ansi-ports)
                          (:paramports . vl-parse-temps->paramports)
                          (:imports . vl-parse-temps->imports)
                          (:loaditems . vl-parse-temps->loaditems))
                        'change-vl-parse-temps
                        'nil))
