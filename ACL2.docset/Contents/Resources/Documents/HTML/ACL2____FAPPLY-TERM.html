Variant of apply-term that performs no simplification.

Signature
(fapply-term fn terms) → term
Arguments
fn — Guard (pseudo-termfnp fn).
terms — Guard (pseudo-term-listp terms).
Returns
term — A pseudo-termp.

The meaning of the starting f in the name of this utility is analogous to fcons-term compared to cons-term.

Function: fapply-term

(defun fapply-term (fn terms)
       (declare (xargs :guard (and (pseudo-termfnp fn)
                                   (pseudo-term-listp terms))))
       (declare (xargs :guard (or (symbolp fn)
                                  (= (len terms)
                                     (len (lambda-formals fn))))))
       (let ((__function__ 'fapply-term))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (cond ((symbolp fn) (fcons-term fn terms))
                  (t (fsubcor-var (lambda-formals fn)
                                  terms (lambda-body fn))))))
