A (possibly synchronous) accept_on or reject_on property expression.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-propexpr.

Fields
op — vl-property-acceptop-p
The kind of operator, e.g., accept_on, reject_on, etc.
condition — vl-exprdist
The abort condition for this operation, e.g., foo in accept_on(foo) bar.
prop — vl-propexpr
The abort property, e.g., bar in accept_on(foo) bar.
