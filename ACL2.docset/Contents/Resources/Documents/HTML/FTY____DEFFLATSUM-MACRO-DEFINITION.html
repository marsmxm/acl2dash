Definition of the defflatsum macro.

Submit the event generated by defflatsum-fn.

Macro: defflatsum

(defmacro defflatsum (&rest args)
          (cons 'make-event
                (cons (cons 'defflatsum-fn
                            (cons (cons 'quote (cons args 'nil))
                                  (cons (cons 'w (cons 'state 'nil))
                                        'nil)))
                      'nil)))
