Function variables depended on by a second-order theorem or by an instance of it.

Signature
(funvars-of-thm thm wrld) → funvars
Arguments
thm — Guard (symbolp thm).
wrld — Guard (plist-worldp wrld).
Returns
funvars — A funvar-listp.

Second-order theorems and their instances may depend on function variables via their formulas.

The returned list may contain duplicates.

(defun funvars-of-thm (thm wrld)
       (declare (xargs :guard (and (symbolp thm)
                                   (plist-worldp wrld))))
       (let ((__function__ 'funvars-of-thm))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (funvars-of-term (formula thm nil wrld)
                             wrld)))
