This function returns the numerator and denominator of a rational approximation to cos(x) (in radians) by itr iterations of fast-compute-series.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: fast-compute-cos

(defun fast-compute-cos (x itr)
       (declare (xargs :guard (and (rationalp x)
                                   (integerp itr)
                                   (>= itr 0))))
       (fast-compute-series (* (numerator x) (numerator x))
                            (* (denominator x) (denominator x))
                            1 1 1 0 nil itr))
