b* binder for freeing fast alists when the b* exits.

This is a b* binder introduced with def-b*-binder.

Macro: patbind-free-on-exit

(defmacro patbind-free-on-exit
          (args forms rest-expr)
          (declare (xargs :guard (not forms))
                   (ignorable forms))
          (cons 'fast-alists-free-on-exit
                (cons args (cons rest-expr 'nil))))
