Pop the current scope off of the lexical scope stack and free fast alists.

Signature
(vl-lexscopes-exit-scope scopes) → scopes
Arguments

This should be called when exiting a module, function, block, etc.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: vl-lexscopes-exit-scope

(defun vl-lexscopes-exit-scope (scopes)
       (declare (xargs :guard (vl-lexscopes-p scopes)))
       (let ((__function__ 'vl-lexscopes-exit-scope))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (b* ((scopes (vl-lexscopes-fix scopes))
                 ((when (atom scopes))
                  (raise "Expected at least one lexscope."))
                 ((cons head tail) scopes))
                (fast-alist-free head)
                tail)))
