Rules about the composition of sint-integer-value with sint-from-<type> functions.

These are not used during the symbolic execution; they are used to prove rules used during the symbolic execution.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: sint-integer-value-of-sint-from-schar

(defthm sint-integer-value-of-sint-from-schar
        (implies (scharp x)
                 (equal (sint-integer-value (sint-from-schar x))
                        (schar-integer-value x))))
