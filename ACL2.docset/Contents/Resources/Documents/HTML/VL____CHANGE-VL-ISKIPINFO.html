Modifying constructor for vl-iskipinfo structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-iskipinfo x 
                     [:controller <controller>] 
                     [:misses <misses>] 
                     [:hits <hits>] 
                     [:len <len>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-iskipinfo.

We construct a new vl-iskipinfo structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-iskipinfo

(defmacro
   change-vl-iskipinfo (x &rest args)
   (std::change-aggregate 'vl-iskipinfo
                          x args
                          '((:controller . vl-iskipinfo->controller)
                            (:misses . vl-iskipinfo->misses)
                            (:hits . vl-iskipinfo->hits)
                            (:len . vl-iskipinfo->len))
                          'change-vl-iskipinfo
                          'remake-vl-iskipinfo))
