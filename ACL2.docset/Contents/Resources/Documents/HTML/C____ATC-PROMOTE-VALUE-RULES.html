Rules about promote-value on values of given types.

These are not used during the symbolic execution; they are used to prove rules used during the symbolic execution.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: promote-value-when-scharp

(defthm promote-value-when-scharp
        (implies (scharp x)
                 (equal (promote-value x)
                        (sint-from-schar x))))

Theorem: promote-value-when-ucharp

(defthm promote-value-when-ucharp
        (implies (ucharp x)
                 (equal (promote-value x)
                        (if (<= (uchar-max) (sint-max))
                            (sint-from-uchar x)
                            (uint-from-uchar x)))))
