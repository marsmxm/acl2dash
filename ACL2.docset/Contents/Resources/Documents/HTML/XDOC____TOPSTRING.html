Construct an XDOC string from a sequence of XDOC trees.

This can be used to construct XDOC strings at the ``top level'', e.g. :short and :long documentation string.

Macro: topstring

(defmacro topstring (&rest trees)
          (cons 'tree-to-string
                (cons (cons '&& trees) 'nil)))
