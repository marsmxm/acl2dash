Syntactic equivalence of faigs under faig-fix.

Two objects are faig-fix-equiv if their faig-fixes are equal.

Function: faig-fix-equiv

(defun faig-fix-equiv (x y)
       (and (equal (faig-fix x) (faig-fix y))))
