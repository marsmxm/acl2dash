Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: unsigned-byte-p-lshu

(defthm unsigned-byte-p-lshu
        (implies (and (>= size1 size)
                      (>= size 0)
                      (integerp size)
                      (integerp size1))
                 (unsigned-byte-p size1 (lshu size i cnt))))
