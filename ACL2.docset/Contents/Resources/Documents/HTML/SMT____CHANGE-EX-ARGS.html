Modifying constructor for ex-args structures.

Syntax
(change-ex-args x 
                [:term-lst <term-lst>] 
                [:fn-lst <fn-lst>] 
                [:fn-lvls <fn-lvls>] 
                [:wrld-fn-len <wrld-fn-len>] 
                [:expand-lst <expand-lst>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-ex-args.

We construct a new ex-args structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-ex-args

(defmacro
     change-ex-args (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'ex-args
                            x args
                            '((:term-lst . ex-args->term-lst)
                              (:fn-lst . ex-args->fn-lst)
                              (:fn-lvls . ex-args->fn-lvls)
                              (:wrld-fn-len . ex-args->wrld-fn-len)
                              (:expand-lst . ex-args->expand-lst))
                            'change-ex-args
                            'nil))
