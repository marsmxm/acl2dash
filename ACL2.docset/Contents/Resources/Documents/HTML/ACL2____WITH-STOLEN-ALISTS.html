Concisely call with-stolen-alist on multiple alists.

Example:

(with-stolen-alists (a b c) form)

is just shorthand for:

(with-stolen-alist a
  (with-stolen-alist b
    (with-stolen-alist c
      form)))

Macro: with-stolen-alists

(defmacro with-stolen-alists (alists form)
          (with-stolen-alists-fn alists form))

Function: with-stolen-alists-fn

(defun with-stolen-alists-fn (alists form)
       (if (atom alists)
           form
           (cons 'with-stolen-alist
                 (cons (car alists)
                       (cons (with-stolen-alists-fn (cdr alists)
                                                    form)
                             'nil)))))
