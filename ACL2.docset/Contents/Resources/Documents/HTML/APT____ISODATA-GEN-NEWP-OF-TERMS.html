Apply the newpk or newp_rk function to the corresponding term in a list of terms of length equal to the number of formals or results of old.

terms — Guard (pseudo-term-listp terms).
arg/res-isomaps — Guard (isodata-symbol-isomap-alistp arg/res-isomaps).
Returns
new-terms — A pseudo-term-listp.

Function: isodata-gen-newp-of-terms

(defun
 isodata-gen-newp-of-terms
 (terms arg/res-isomaps)
 (declare
  (xargs
       :guard (and (pseudo-term-listp terms)
                   (isodata-symbol-isomap-alistp arg/res-isomaps))))
 (declare (xargs :guard (= (len terms) (len arg/res-isomaps))))
 (let
  ((__function__ 'isodata-gen-newp-of-terms))
  (declare (ignorable __function__))
  (b*
     (((when (endp terms)) nil)
      (term (car terms))
      (isomap (cdar arg/res-isomaps))
      (newp (isodata-isomap->newp isomap))
      (new-term (apply-term* newp term))
      (new-terms (isodata-gen-newp-of-terms (cdr terms)
                                            (cdr arg/res-isomaps))))
     (cons new-term new-terms))))
