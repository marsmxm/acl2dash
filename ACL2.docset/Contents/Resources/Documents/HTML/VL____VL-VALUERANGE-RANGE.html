A range of values from an inside expression's set. For instance, the [16:20] part of a inside { 8, [16:20] }.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-valuerange.

Fields
low — vl-expr
Always the left component, e.g., 16 in [16:20].
high — vl-expr
Always the high component, e.g., 20 in [16:20].
