Modifying constructor for vl-structmember structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-structmember x 
                        [:type <type>] 
                        [:name <name>] 
                        [:rhs <rhs>] 
                        [:rand <rand>] 
                        [:atts <atts>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-structmember.

We construct a new vl-structmember structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-structmember

(defmacro change-vl-structmember (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-structmember
                                 x args
                                 '((:type . vl-structmember->type)
                                   (:name . vl-structmember->name)
                                   (:rhs . vl-structmember->rhs)
                                   (:rand . vl-structmember->rand)
                                   (:atts . vl-structmember->atts))
                                 'change-vl-structmember
                                 'remake-vl-structmember))
