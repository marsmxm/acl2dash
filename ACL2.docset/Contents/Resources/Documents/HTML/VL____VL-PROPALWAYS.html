An always or s_always property expression.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-propexpr.

Fields
strongp — booleanp
True when this is an s_always.
range — vl-maybe-range
The range applied to this always if applicable. For instance, in always [2:3] foo, the range is [2:3]. A plain always may or may not have a range, but an s_always should always have one. We don't enforce this in our data structures, but it is enforced by the parser.
prop — vl-propexpr
The property being tested, e.g., foo in always foo.
