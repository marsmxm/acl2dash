Identical to 4v-sexpr-compose, but not memoized and does not use fast alist lookups.

Used for rewriting because the terms and alists involved are small. This is called on the RHS of a rewrite rule, which is a small term in common usage; with an alist that may have large terms in its range, but only has a few variables bound. Therefore, it is appropriate to forego memoization and fast alist lookups, since the overhead will outweigh the benefit.
