(cdr-cdr-induct x y) inducts by simultaneously cdr'ing x and y until we reach the end of either.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: cdr-cdr-induct

(defun cdr-cdr-induct (x y)
       (if (or (atom x) (atom y))
           nil (cdr-cdr-induct (cdr x) (cdr y))))
