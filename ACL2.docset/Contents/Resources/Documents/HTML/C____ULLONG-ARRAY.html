Additional Requirements

The following invariant is enforced on the fields:

(and (type-case elemtype :ullong) (consp elements))

Theorem: ullong-arrayp-alt-def

(defthm ullong-arrayp-alt-def
        (equal (ullong-arrayp x)
               (and (valuep x)
                    (value-case x :array)
                    (equal (value-array->elemtype x)
                           (type-ullong))
                    (ullong-listp (value-array->elements x)))))

Theorem: ullong-array->elements-alt-def

(defthm ullong-array->elements-alt-def
        (implies (ullong-arrayp array)
                 (equal (ullong-array->elements array)
                        (value-array->elements array))))
