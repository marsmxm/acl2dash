A repetition is well-formed iff its repetition range and its element are well-formed.

Signature
(repetition-wfp repetition) → yes/no
Arguments
repetition — Guard (repetitionp repetition).
Returns
yes/no — Type (booleanp yes/no).

This condition is structural.

Function: repetition-wfp

(defun repetition-wfp (repetition)
       (declare (xargs :guard (repetitionp repetition)))
       (and (repeat-range-wfp (repetition->range repetition))
            (element-wfp (repetition->element repetition))))
