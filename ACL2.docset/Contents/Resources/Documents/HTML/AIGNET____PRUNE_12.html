Like prune, but overwrites the original network instead of returning a new one.

Signature
(prune! aignet config) → new-aignet
Arguments
aignet — Input aignet -- will be replaced with transformation result.
config — Guard (prune-config-p config).

Definitions and Theorems

Function: prune!

(defun
 prune! (aignet config)
 (declare (xargs :stobjs (aignet)))
 (declare (xargs :guard (prune-config-p config)))
 (let
  ((__function__ 'prune!))
  (declare (ignorable __function__))
  (b* (((local-stobjs aignet-tmp)
        (mv aignet aignet-tmp))
       (aignet-tmp (aignet-raw-copy aignet aignet-tmp))
       (aignet (aignet-prune-comb aignet-tmp aignet
                                  (prune-config->gatesimp config))))
      (mv aignet aignet-tmp))))
