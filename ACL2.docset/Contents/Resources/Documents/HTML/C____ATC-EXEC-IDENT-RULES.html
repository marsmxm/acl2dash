Rules for executing identifiers.

To symbolically execute an identifier (as an expression), we use a rule that is like the definition, but provides a bit of separation/abstraction from the definition, and it also avoids the binding of __function__. The read-var call may undergo further rewriting, as explained in atc-read-var-rules.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: exec-ident-open

(defthm exec-ident-open
        (equal (exec-ident id compst)
               (read-var id compst)))
