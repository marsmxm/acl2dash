A term parse tree, where term is a terminal numeric or character value notation, is generally abstracted to its list of natural numbers.

Signature
(abstract-terminals tree) → nats
Arguments
tree — Guard (treep tree).
Returns
nats — Type (nat-listp nats).

The parse tree must be a leaf labeled by a list of natural numbers. That list of natural numbers is returned.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: abstract-terminals

(defun abstract-terminals (tree)
       (declare (xargs :guard (treep tree)))
       (if (tree-case tree :leafterm)
           (tree-leafterm->get tree)
           (abstract-fail)))

(defthm nat-listp-of-abstract-terminals
        (b* ((nats (abstract-terminals tree)))
