Four-valued semantics for not gates.

(4v-not a) returns the value specified by the following truth table:

  a  |  out
-----+-------
  T  |   F
  F  |   T
  X  |   X
  Z  |   X
-----+-------

See 4v-unfloat for an explanation of the Z case.
