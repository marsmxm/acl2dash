Modifying constructor for sd-problem structures.

Syntax
(change-sd-problem x 
                   [:type <type>] 
                   [:priority <priority>] 
                   [:groupsize <groupsize>] 
                   [:key <key>] 
                   [:ctx <ctx>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-sd-problem.

We construct a new sd-problem structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-sd-problem

(defmacro
     change-sd-problem (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'sd-problem
                            x args
                            '((:type . sd-problem->type)
                              (:priority . sd-problem->priority)
                              (:groupsize . sd-problem->groupsize)
                              (:key . sd-problem->key)
                              (:ctx . sd-problem->ctx))
                            'change-sd-problem
                            'remake-sd-problem))
