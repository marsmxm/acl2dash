Recognize printable ASCII characters, i.e. spaces and visible ASCII characters.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: printable-char-p

(defun printable-char-p (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (and (characterp x)
            (b* ((code (char-code x)))
                (and (<= 32 code) (<= code 126)))))

Theorem: booleanp-of-printable-char-p

(defthm booleanp-of-printable-char-p
        (booleanp (printable-char-p x))
        :rule-classes :type-prescription)

Theorem: characterp-when-printable-char-p

(defthm characterp-when-printable-char-p
        (implies (printable-char-p x)
                 (characterp x))
        :rule-classes :compound-recognizer)
