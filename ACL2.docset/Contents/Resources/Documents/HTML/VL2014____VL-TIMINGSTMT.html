Representation of timing statements.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-stmt.

Fields
ctrl — vl-delayoreventcontrol
body — vl-stmt
atts — vl-atts

General Form:

<ctrl> <body>

Examples:

#3 foo = bar;
@@(posedge clk) foo = bar;
@@(bar or baz) foo = bar | baz;
