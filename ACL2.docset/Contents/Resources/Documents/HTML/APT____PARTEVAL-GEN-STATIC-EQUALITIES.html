Generate the equalities whose conjunction forms the antecedent of the theorem relating old and new function.

Signature
(parteval-gen-static-equalities static$) → equalities
Arguments
static$ — Guard (symbol-alistp static$).
Returns
equalities — A pseudo-term-listp.

Each equality has the form (equal yj cj).
