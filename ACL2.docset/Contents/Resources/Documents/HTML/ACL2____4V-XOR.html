Four-valued semantics for xor gates.

(4v-xor a b) returns:

  • T when its inputs are Boolean and differ, or
  • F when its inputs are Boolean and are the same, or
  • X otherwise.

See 4v-unfloat for an explanation of the Z case.

Function: 4v-xor$inline

(defun 4v-xor$inline (a b)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (mbe :logic (4vcases a (t (4v-not b))
                            (f (4v-unfloat b))
                            (& (4vx)))
            :exec (cond ((eq a (4vt))
                         (cond ((eq b (4vt)) (4vf))
                               ((eq b (4vf)) (4vt))
                               (t (4vx))))
                        ((eq a (4vf))
                         (cond ((eq b (4vt)) (4vt))
                               ((eq b (4vf)) (4vf))
                               (t (4vx))))
                        (t (4vx)))))
