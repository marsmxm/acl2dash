Recognizer for integers between two bounds.

The predicate (integer-range-p lower upper x) tests whether x is an integer greater than or equal to lower and less than upper.

Function: integer-range-p

(defun integer-range-p (lower upper x)
       (declare (type integer lower upper))
       (and (integerp x)
            (<= lower x)
            (< x upper)))
