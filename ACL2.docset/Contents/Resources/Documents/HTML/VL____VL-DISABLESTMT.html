Representation of a disable statement.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-stmt.

Fields
id — vl-scopeexpr
atts — vl-atts
Any (* foo, bar = 1*) style attributes associated with this statement.

Disable statements are simpler and just have a hierarchial identifier. Apparently there are no disable statements for system identifiers.
