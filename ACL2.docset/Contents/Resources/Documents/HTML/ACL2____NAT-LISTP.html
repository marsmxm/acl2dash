Recognizer for a true list of natural numbers

The predicate nat-listp tests whether its argument is a true list of natural numbers.

Function: nat-listp

(defun nat-listp (l)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (cond ((atom l) (eq l nil))
             (t (and (natp (car l))
                     (nat-listp (cdr l))))))
