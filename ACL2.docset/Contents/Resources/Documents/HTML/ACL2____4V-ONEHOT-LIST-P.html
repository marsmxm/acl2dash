(4v-onehot-list-p x) determines if a list of 4vps has exactly one member that is T while the rest are F.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: 4v-onehot-list-p

(defun 4v-onehot-list-p (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (if (atom x)
           nil
           (4vcases (car x)
                    (f (4v-onehot-list-p (cdr x)))
                    (t (all-equalp *4vf* (cdr x)))
                    (& nil))))

Theorem: 4v-onehot-list-p-when-atom

(defthm 4v-onehot-list-p-when-atom
        (implies (atom x)
                 (equal (4v-onehot-list-p x) nil)))

Theorem: 4v-onehot-list-p-of-cons

(defthm 4v-onehot-list-p-of-cons
        (equal (4v-onehot-list-p (cons a x))
               (4vcases a (f (4v-onehot-list-p x))
                        (t (all-equalp *4vf* x))
                        (& nil))))
