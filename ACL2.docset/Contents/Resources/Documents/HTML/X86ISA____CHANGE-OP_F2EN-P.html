Modifying constructor for op/en-p structures.

Syntax
(change-op/en-p x 
                [:src1 <src1>] 
                [:src2 <src2>] 
                [:src3 <src3>] 
                [:src4 <src4>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-op/en-p.

We construct a new op/en-p structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-op/en-p

(defmacro change-op/en-p (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'op/en-p
                                 x args
                                 '((:src1 . op/en-p->src1)
                                   (:src2 . op/en-p->src2)
                                   (:src3 . op/en-p->src3)
                                   (:src4 . op/en-p->src4))
                                 'change-op/en-p
                                 'nil))
