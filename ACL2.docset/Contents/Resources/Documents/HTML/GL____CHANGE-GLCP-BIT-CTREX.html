A copying macro that lets you create new glcp-bit-ctrex-p structures, based on existing structures.

Syntax:

(change-glcp-bit-ctrex x 
                       [:descrip <descrip>] 
                       [:env <env>] 
                       [:gvar-alist <gvar-alist>] 
                       [:dont-care-spec <dont-care-spec>])

This is a sometimes useful alternative to make-glcp-bit-ctrex. It constructs a new glcp-bit-ctrex-p structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by std::defaggregate.

Macro: change-glcp-bit-ctrex

(defmacro change-glcp-bit-ctrex (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'glcp-bit-ctrex
               x args
               '((:descrip . glcp-bit-ctrex->descrip)
                 (:env . glcp-bit-ctrex->env)
                 (:gvar-alist . glcp-bit-ctrex->gvar-alist)
                 (:dont-care-spec . glcp-bit-ctrex->dont-care-spec))
               'change-glcp-bit-ctrex
               'remake-glcp-bit-ctrex))
