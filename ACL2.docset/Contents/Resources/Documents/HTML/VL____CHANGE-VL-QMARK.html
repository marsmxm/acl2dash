Modifying constructor for vl-qmark structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-qmark x 
                 [:test <test>] 
                 [:then <then>] 
                 [:else <else>] 
                 [:atts <atts>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-qmark.

We construct a new vl-qmark structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-qmark

(defmacro change-vl-qmark (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-qmark
                                 x args
                                 '((:test . vl-qmark->test)
                                   (:then . vl-qmark->then)
                                   (:else . vl-qmark->else)
                                   (:atts . vl-qmark->atts))
                                 'change-vl-qmark
                                 'remake-vl-qmark))
