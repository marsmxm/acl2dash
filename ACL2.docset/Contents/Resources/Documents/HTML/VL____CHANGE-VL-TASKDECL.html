Modifying constructor for vl-taskdecl structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-taskdecl x 
                    [:name <name>] 
                    [:body <body>] 
                    [:loc <loc>] 
                    [:portdecls <portdecls>] 
                    [:vardecls <vardecls>] 
                    [:paramdecls <paramdecls>] 
                    [:typedefs <typedefs>] 
                    [:imports <imports>] 
                    [:atts <atts>] 
                    [:lifetime <lifetime>] 
                    [:loaditems <loaditems>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-taskdecl.

We construct a new vl-taskdecl structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-taskdecl

(defmacro
    change-vl-taskdecl (x &rest args)
    (std::change-aggregate 'vl-taskdecl
                           x args
                           '((:name . vl-taskdecl->name)
                             (:body . vl-taskdecl->body)
                             (:loc . vl-taskdecl->loc)
                             (:portdecls . vl-taskdecl->portdecls)
                             (:vardecls . vl-taskdecl->vardecls)
                             (:paramdecls . vl-taskdecl->paramdecls)
                             (:typedefs . vl-taskdecl->typedefs)
                             (:imports . vl-taskdecl->imports)
                             (:atts . vl-taskdecl->atts)
                             (:lifetime . vl-taskdecl->lifetime)
                             (:loaditems . vl-taskdecl->loaditems))
                           'change-vl-taskdecl
                           'remake-vl-taskdecl))
