Modifying constructor for vl-clkassign structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-clkassign x 
                     [:name <name>] 
                     [:loc <loc>] 
                     [:inputp <inputp>] 
                     [:rhs <rhs>] 
                     [:skew <skew>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-clkassign.

We construct a new vl-clkassign structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-clkassign

(defmacro change-vl-clkassign (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-clkassign
                                 x args
                                 '((:name . vl-clkassign->name)
                                   (:loc . vl-clkassign->loc)
                                   (:inputp . vl-clkassign->inputp)
                                   (:rhs . vl-clkassign->rhs)
                                   (:skew . vl-clkassign->skew))
                                 'change-vl-clkassign
                                 'remake-vl-clkassign))
