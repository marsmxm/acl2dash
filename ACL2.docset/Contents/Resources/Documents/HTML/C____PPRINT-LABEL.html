We also print a space just after the colon, so that the sub-statement after the label is separated by a space.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: pprint-label

(defun pprint-label (lab)
       (declare (xargs :guard (labelp lab)))
       (let ((__function__ 'pprint-label))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (label-case lab
                        :name (msg "~@0: " (pprint-ident lab.get))
                        :cas "case: "
                        :default "default: ")))

Theorem: msgp-of-pprint-label

(defthm msgp-of-pprint-label
        (b* ((part (pprint-label lab)))
            (msgp part))
        :rule-classes :rewrite)

(defthm pprint-label-of-label-fix-lab
