Modifying constructor for vl-unparam-ledger structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-unparam-ledger x 
                          [:ndb <ndb>] 
                          [:instkeymap <instkeymap>] 
                          [:omit-default-params <omit-default-params>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-unparam-ledger.

We construct a new vl-unparam-ledger structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-unparam-ledger

(defmacro
 change-vl-unparam-ledger (x &rest args)
 (std::change-aggregate
    'vl-unparam-ledger
    x args
    '((:ndb . vl-unparam-ledger->ndb)
      (:instkeymap . vl-unparam-ledger->instkeymap)
      (:omit-default-params .
                            vl-unparam-ledger->omit-default-params))
    'change-vl-unparam-ledger
    'nil))
