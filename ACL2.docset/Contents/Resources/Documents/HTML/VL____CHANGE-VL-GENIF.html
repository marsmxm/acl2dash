Modifying constructor for vl-genif structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-genif x 
                 [:test <test>] 
                 [:then <then>] 
                 [:else <else>] 
                 [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-genif.

We construct a new vl-genif structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-genif

(defmacro change-vl-genif (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-genif
                                 x args
                                 '((:test . vl-genif->test)
                                   (:then . vl-genif->then)
                                   (:else . vl-genif->else)
                                   (:loc . vl-genif->loc))
                                 'change-vl-genif
                                 'remake-vl-genif))
