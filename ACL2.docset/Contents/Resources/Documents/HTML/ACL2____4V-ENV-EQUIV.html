Equivalence of four-valued environments (alists).

X === Y in the sense of four-valued environments if X[k] === Y[k] for all keys k, in the sense of 4v-equiv.

Function: 4v-env-equiv

(defun 4v-env-equiv (x y)
       (declare (xargs :non-executable t))
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (declare (xargs :non-executable t))
       (prog2$ (throw-nonexec-error '4v-env-equiv
                                    (list x y))
               (let ((key (4v-env-equiv-witness x y)))
                    (and (equal (4v-lookup key x)
                                (4v-lookup key y))))))
