Modifying constructor for vl-assertion structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-assertion x 
                     [:name <name>] 
                     [:type <type>] 
                     [:deferral <deferral>] 
                     [:condition <condition>] 
                     [:success <success>] 
                     [:failure <failure>] 
                     [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-assertion.

We construct a new vl-assertion structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-assertion

(defmacro
     change-vl-assertion (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'vl-assertion
                            x args
                            '((:name . vl-assertion->name)
                              (:type . vl-assertion->type)
                              (:deferral . vl-assertion->deferral)
                              (:condition . vl-assertion->condition)
                              (:success . vl-assertion->success)
                              (:failure . vl-assertion->failure)
                              (:loc . vl-assertion->loc))
                            'change-vl-assertion
                            'remake-vl-assertion))
