Direction for a port declaration (input, output, or inout).

Each port declaration (see vl-portdecl-p) includes a direction to indicate that the port is either an input, output, or inout. We represent these directions with the keyword :vl-input, :vl-output, and :vl-inout, respectively.

In our argresolve transformation, directions are also assigned to all arguments of gate instances and most arguments of module instances. See our vl-plainarg-p structures for more information.

This is an ordinary defenum.

Function: vl-direction-p

(defun vl-direction-p (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (or (eq x ':vl-input)
           (eq x ':vl-output)
           (eq x ':vl-inout)))

(defthm type-when-vl-direction-p
        (implies (vl-direction-p x)
                 (if (symbolp x)
                     (if (not (equal x 't))
                         (not (equal x 'nil))
                         'nil)
                     'nil))
