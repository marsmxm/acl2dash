A list of lists of trees matches an alternation iff it matches one of its concatenations.

Function: tree-list-list-match-alternation-p

(defun
 tree-list-list-match-alternation-p
 (treess alternation rules)
 (declare (xargs :guard (and (tree-list-listp treess)
                             (alternationp alternation)
                             (rulelistp rules))))
 (and
  (consp alternation)
  (or (tree-list-list-match-concatenation-p treess (car alternation)
                                            rules)
      (tree-list-list-match-alternation-p treess (cdr alternation)
                                          rules))))
