An indexed occurrence of an identifier.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-lucidocc.

Fields
left — vl-expr
right — vl-expr
ctx — vl-lucidctx
The general context where the usage occurred. Knowing where the occurrence came from is useful when we need to report about multiply driven signals.

We use this to record occurrences of an identifier with a bit- or part-select. For individual selects like foo[3], both left and right will be the same.
