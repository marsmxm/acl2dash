Remaining characters in a strin-p.

Signature
(strin-left x) → chars
Arguments
x — Guard (strin-p x).
Returns
chars — Type (character-listp chars).

This is an alternative to strin->chars that charlist-fixes the characters. This is generally preferable to using strin->chars directly, and allows us to avoid strin-p hyps in many theorems.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: strin-left

(defun strin-left (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard (strin-p x)))
       (let ((__function__ 'strin-left))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (mbe :logic (charlist-fix (strin->chars x))
                 :exec (strin->chars x))))

Theorem: character-listp-of-strin-left

(defthm character-listp-of-strin-left
        (b* ((chars (strin-left x)))
            (character-listp chars))
        :rule-classes :rewrite)
