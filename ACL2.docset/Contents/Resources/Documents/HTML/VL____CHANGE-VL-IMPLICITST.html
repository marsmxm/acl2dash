Modifying constructor for vl-implicitst structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-implicitst x 
                      [:portdecls <portdecls>] 
                      [:decls <decls>] 
                      [:imports <imports>] 
                      [:ss <ss>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-implicitst.

We construct a new vl-implicitst structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-implicitst

(defmacro
    change-vl-implicitst (x &rest args)
    (std::change-aggregate 'vl-implicitst

                             (:decls . vl-implicitst->decls)
                             (:imports . vl-implicitst->imports)
                             (:ss . vl-implicitst->ss))
                           'change-vl-implicitst
