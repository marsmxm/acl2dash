Recogize characters that are okay in the "tails" of identifiers: letters, numbers, and underscores.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: idtail-char-p

(defun idtail-char-p (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (and (characterp x)
            (or (letter-char-p x)
                (number-char-p x)
                (eql x #\_))))
