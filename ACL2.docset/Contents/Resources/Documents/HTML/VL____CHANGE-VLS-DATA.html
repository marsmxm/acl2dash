A copying macro that lets you create new vls-data-p structures, based on existing structures.

Syntax:

(change-vls-data x 
                 [:orig <orig>] 
                 [:name <name>] 
                 [:date <date>] 
                 [:ltime <ltime>] 
                 [:orig-descalist <orig-descalist>] 
                 [:filemap <filemap>] 
                 [:defs <defs>])

This is a sometimes useful alternative to make-vls-data. It constructs a new vls-data-p structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defaggregate.

Macro: change-vls-data

(defmacro change-vls-data (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'vls-data
               x args
               '((:orig . vls-data->orig)
                 (:name . vls-data->name)
                 (:date . vls-data->date)
                 (:ltime . vls-data->ltime)
                 (:orig-descalist . vls-data->orig-descalist)
                 (:filemap . vls-data->filemap)
                 (:defs . vls-data->defs))
               'change-vls-data
               'nil))
