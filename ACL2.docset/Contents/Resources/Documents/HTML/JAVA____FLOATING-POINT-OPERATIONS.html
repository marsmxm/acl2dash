Java floating-point operations [JLS:4.2.4].

Here we provide abstract notions for all the unary and binary operations on floating-point values that are not conversions (those are formalized separately).

[JLS:4.2.4] also lists the prefix and posfix ++ and -- operators, but those operate on variables, not just values, and therefore must be formalized elsewhere.

[JLS:4.2.4] also lists the conditional operator ? :, but that one is non-strict, and therefore must be formalized as part of expression evaluation.

[JLS:4.2.4] also lists the string concatenation operator +, but that is best formalized in terms of integral-to-string conversions, elsewhere.

[JLS:4.2.4] also lists the cast operator, which involves conversions, which, as mentioned above, are formalized separately.
