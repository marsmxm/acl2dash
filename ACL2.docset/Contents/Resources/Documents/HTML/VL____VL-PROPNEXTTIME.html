A nexttime or s_nexttime property expression.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-propexpr.

Fields
strongp — booleanp
True for s_nexttime operators, nil for ordinary nexttime.
expr — vl-maybe-expr
For instance, #('3') in case of nexttime [3] foo.
prop — vl-propexpr
The property that must hold next time, e.g., foo in nexttime [3] foo.
