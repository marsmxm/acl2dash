Technical lemmas to show that opening input/output channels does not produce a symbol like ACL2-OUTPUT-CHANNEL::STANDARD-CHARACTER-OUTPUT-0, for the guards of close-input-channel and close-output-channel.

Theorem: print-base-p-implies-posp

(defthm print-base-p-implies-posp
        (implies (print-base-p x) (posp x))
        :rule-classes :forward-chaining)

(defthm print-base-p-bound

        :rule-classes :forward-chaining)

Theorem: make-output-channel-not-standard-co

(defthm make-output-channel-not-standard-co
        (implies (posp clock)
                 (not (equal (make-output-channel str clock)
                             *standard-co*))))
