Turn a field into a typed variable.

Signature
(field-to-typed-variable field) → var
Arguments
field — Guard (fieldp field).
Returns
var — Type (typed-variablep var).

Sometimes fields are used as variables, in particular in invariants of product and sum types. Fields and typed variables are isomorphic. This function converts the former to the latter.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: field-to-typed-variable

(defun field-to-typed-variable (field)
       (declare (xargs :guard (fieldp field)))
       (let ((__function__ 'field-to-typed-variable))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (make-typed-variable :name (field->name field)
                                 :type (field->type field))))

Theorem: typed-variablep-of-field-to-typed-variable

(defthm typed-variablep-of-field-to-typed-variable
        (b* ((var (field-to-typed-variable field)))
            (typed-variablep var))
        :rule-classes :rewrite)

(defthm field-to-typed-variable-of-field-fix-field
        (equal (field-to-typed-variable (field-fix field))
               (field-to-typed-variable field)))
