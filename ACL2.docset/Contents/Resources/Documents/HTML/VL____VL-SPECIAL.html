Representation of a few special things like $, null, etc.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-expr.

Fields
key — vl-specialkey-p
atts — vl-atts
Any attribute associated with this expression. As with literals, these attributes are not accessible in the Verilog or SystemVerilog grammars. However, it is generally convenient to be able to associate attributes with any expression, so we include an attributes field in our internal representation.
