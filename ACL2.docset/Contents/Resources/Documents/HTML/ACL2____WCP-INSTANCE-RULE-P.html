(wcp-instance-rule-p x) is a std::defaggregate of the following fields.

  • name — Invariant (symbolp name).
  • enabledp
  • pred — Invariant (pseudo-termp pred).
  • vars — Invariant (and (symbol-listp vars) (not (intersectp-equal vars (simple-term-vars pred)))) .
  • expr — Invariant (pseudo-termp expr).
  • restriction — Invariant (pseudo-termp restriction).
  • theorem — Invariant (symbolp theorem).
