Modifying constructor for vl-coretype structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-coretype x 
                    [:name <name>] 
                    [:signedp <signedp>] 
                    [:pdims <pdims>] 
                    [:udims <udims>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-coretype.

We construct a new vl-coretype structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-coretype

(defmacro change-vl-coretype (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-coretype
                                 x args
                                 '((:name . vl-coretype->name)
                                   (:signedp . vl-coretype->signedp)
                                   (:pdims . vl-coretype->pdims)
                                   (:udims . vl-coretype->udims))
                                 'change-vl-coretype
                                 'remake-vl-coretype))
