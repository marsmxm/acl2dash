Check if two lists of statements are related by variable renaming.

Signature
(statement-list-renamevar old new ren) → new-ren
Arguments
old — Guard (statement-listp old).
new — Guard (statement-listp new).
ren — Guard (renamingp ren).
Returns
new-ren — Type (renaming-resultp new-ren).

The two lists must have the same length, and have corresponding elements related by the renaming. The renaming list is updated and threaded through the statements.
