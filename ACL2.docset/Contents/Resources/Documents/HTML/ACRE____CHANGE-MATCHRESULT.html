Modifying constructor for matchresult structures.

Syntax
(change-matchresult x 
                    [:loc <loc>] 
                    [:len <len>] 
                    [:str <str>] 
                    [:backrefs <backrefs>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-matchresult.

We construct a new matchresult structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-matchresult

(defmacro
     change-matchresult (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'matchresult
                            x args
                            '((:loc . matchresult->loc)
                              (:len . matchresult->len)
                              (:str . matchresult->str)
                              (:backrefs . matchresult->backrefs))
                            'change-matchresult
                            'nil))
