Modifying constructor for vl-parsed-ports structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-parsed-ports x 
                        [:ansi-p <ansi-p>] 
                        [:ports <ports>] 
                        [:portdecls <portdecls>] 
                        [:vardecls <vardecls>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-parsed-ports.

We construct a new vl-parsed-ports structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-parsed-ports

(defmacro
  change-vl-parsed-ports (x &rest args)
  (std::change-aggregate 'vl-parsed-ports
                         x args
                         '((:ansi-p . vl-parsed-ports->ansi-p)
                           (:ports . vl-parsed-ports->ports)
                           (:portdecls . vl-parsed-ports->portdecls)
                           (:vardecls . vl-parsed-ports->vardecls))
                         'change-vl-parsed-ports
                         'remake-vl-parsed-ports))
