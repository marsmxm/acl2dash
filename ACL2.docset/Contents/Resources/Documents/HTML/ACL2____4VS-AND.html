(4vs-and a b) constructs a 4v-sexpr equivalent to (and a b).

Definitions and Theorems

Function: 4vs-and

(defun 4vs-and (a b)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (cond ((or (atom a) (atom b))
              (cond ((equal a b) (4vs-buf a))
                    (t (hons-list 'and a b))))
             ((4vf-sexpr-p a) a)
             ((4vf-sexpr-p b) b)
             ((4vt-sexpr-p a) (4vs-buf b))
             ((4vt-sexpr-p b) (4vs-buf a))
             ((hons-equal a b) (4vs-buf a))
             (t (hons-list 'and a b))))

Theorem: 4v-sexpr-eval-of-4vs-and

(defthm 4v-sexpr-eval-of-4vs-and
        (equal (4v-sexpr-eval (4vs-and a b) env)
               (4v-and (4v-sexpr-eval a env)
                       (4v-sexpr-eval b env))))

Theorem: 4v-sexpr-vars-of-4vs-and

(defthm 4v-sexpr-vars-of-4vs-and
        (subsetp-equal (4v-sexpr-vars (4vs-and a b))
                       (append (4v-sexpr-vars a)
                               (4v-sexpr-vars b))))
