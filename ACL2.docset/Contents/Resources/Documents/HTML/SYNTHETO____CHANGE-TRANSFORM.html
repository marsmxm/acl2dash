Modifying constructor for transform structures.

Syntax
(change-transform x 
                  [:new-function-name <new-function-name>] 
                  [:old-function-name <old-function-name>] 
                  [:transform-name <transform-name>] 
                  [:arguments <arguments>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-transform.

We construct a new transform structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by fty::defprod.

Macro: change-transform

(defmacro change-transform (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'transform
               x args
               '((:new-function-name . transform->new-function-name)
                 (:old-function-name . transform->old-function-name)
                 (:transform-name . transform->transform-name)
                 (:arguments . transform->arguments))
               'change-transform
               'nil))
