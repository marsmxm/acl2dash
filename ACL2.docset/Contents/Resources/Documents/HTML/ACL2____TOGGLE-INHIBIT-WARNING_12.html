Toggle an inhibit-warnings-table entry non-locally

Please see toggle-inhibit-warning, which is the same as toggle-inhibit-warning! except that the latter is not local to the encapsulate or the book in which it occurs. Probably toggle-inhibit-warning is to be preferred unless you have a good reason for wanting to export the effect of this event outside the enclosing encapsulate or book.
