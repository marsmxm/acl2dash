Property that negation is left and right inverse for addition.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: montgomery-add-of-neg-left

(defthm montgomery-add-of-neg-left
        (implies (point-on-montgomery-p point curve)
                 (equal (montgomery-add (montgomery-neg point curve)
                                        point curve)
                        (montgomery-zero))))
