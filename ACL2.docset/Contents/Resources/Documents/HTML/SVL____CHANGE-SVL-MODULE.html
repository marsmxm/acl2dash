Modifying constructor for svl-module structures.

Syntax
(change-svl-module x 
                   [:rank <rank>] 
                   [:inputs <inputs>] 
                   [:delayed-inputs <delayed-inputs>] 
                   [:outputs <outputs>] 
                   [:occs <occs>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-svl-module.

We construct a new svl-module structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by fty::defprod.

Macro: change-svl-module

(defmacro change-svl-module (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'svl-module
               x args
               '((:rank . svl-module->rank)
                 (:inputs . svl-module->inputs)
                 (:delayed-inputs . svl-module->delayed-inputs)
                 (:outputs . svl-module->outputs)
                 (:occs . svl-module->occs))
               'change-svl-module
               'nil))
