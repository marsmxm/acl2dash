Equivalence of four-valued constants, i.e., 4vps.

X === Y in the sense of four-valued objects if they fix to the same 4vp. That is, except for (4vf), (4vz), and (4vt), all objects are equivalent to (4vx) under this relationship.

Function: 4v-equiv

(defun 4v-equiv (x y)
       (and (equal (4v-fix x) (4v-fix y))))

Theorem: 4v-equiv-is-an-equivalence

(defthm 4v-equiv-is-an-equivalence
        (and (booleanp (4v-equiv x y))
             (4v-equiv x x)
             (implies (4v-equiv x y) (4v-equiv y x))
             (implies (and (4v-equiv x y) (4v-equiv y z))
                      (4v-equiv x z)))
        :rule-classes (:equivalence))
