Test if curve parameters are valid.

(weierstrass-elliptic-curve-p p a b) tests whether the Short Weierstrass curve defined by p, a, and b is a valid elliptic curve.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: weierstrass-elliptic-curve-p

(defun weierstrass-elliptic-curve-p (p a b)
       (and (rtl::primep p)
            (< 3 p)
            (fep a p)
            (fep b p)
            (posp (mod (+ (* 4 a a a) (* 27 b b)) p))))
