Modifying constructor for vl-scopeinfo structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-scopeinfo x 
                     [:locals <locals>] 
                     [:imports <imports>] 
                     [:star-packages <star-packages>] 
                     [:id <id>] 
                     [:scopetype <scopetype>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-scopeinfo.

We construct a new vl-scopeinfo structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-scopeinfo

(defmacro change-vl-scopeinfo (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'vl-scopeinfo
               x args
               '((:locals . vl-scopeinfo->locals)
                 (:imports . vl-scopeinfo->imports)
                 (:star-packages . vl-scopeinfo->star-packages)
                 (:id . vl-scopeinfo->id)
                 (:scopetype . vl-scopeinfo->scopetype))
               'change-vl-scopeinfo
               'remake-vl-scopeinfo))
