Four-valued semantics for or gates.

(4v-or a b) returns:

  • T when either input is T, or
  • X when either input is X/Z, or
  • F when both inputs are F.

See 4v-unfloat for an explanation of the Z case.

(defun 4v-or$inline (a b)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (mbe :logic (4vcases a (t (4vt))
                            (f (4v-unfloat b))
                            (& (4vcases b (t (4vt)) (& (4vx)))))
            :exec (cond ((or (eq a (4vt)) (eq b (4vt))) (4vt))

                        (t (4vx)))))
