Modifying constructor for vl-gencase structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-gencase x 
                   [:test <test>] 
                   [:cases <cases>] 
                   [:default <default>] 
                   [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-gencase.

We construct a new vl-gencase structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-gencase

(defmacro change-vl-gencase (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-gencase
                                 x args
                                 '((:test . vl-gencase->test)
                                   (:cases . vl-gencase->cases)
                                   (:default . vl-gencase->default)
                                   (:loc . vl-gencase->loc))
                                 'change-vl-gencase
                                 'remake-vl-gencase))
