Modifying constructor for vl-unparam-signature structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-unparam-signature x 
                             [:modname <modname>] 
                             [:newname <newname>] 
                             [:final-params <final-params>] 
                             [:final-ports <final-ports>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-unparam-signature.

We construct a new vl-unparam-signature structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-unparam-signature

(defmacro
     change-vl-unparam-signature
     (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate
          'vl-unparam-signature
          x args
          '((:modname . vl-unparam-signature->modname)
            (:newname . vl-unparam-signature->newname)
            (:final-params . vl-unparam-signature->final-params)
            (:final-ports . vl-unparam-signature->final-ports))
          'change-vl-unparam-signature
          'remake-vl-unparam-signature))
