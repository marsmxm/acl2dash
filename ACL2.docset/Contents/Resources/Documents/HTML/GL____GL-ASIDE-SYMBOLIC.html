Alternative to gl-aside that is only evaluated during GL symbolic execution.

Macro: gl-aside-symbolic

(defmacro gl-aside-symbolic (form)
          (cons 'mbe
                (cons ':logic
                      (cons (cons 'gl-aside (cons form 'nil))
                            (cons ':exec (cons 'nil 'nil))))))
