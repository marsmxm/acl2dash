Determine if a resolved vl-range is in ``reverse'' order, i.e., if its msb < lsb.

Function: vl-range-revp

(defun vl-range-revp (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard (and (vl-range-p x)
                                   (vl-range-resolved-p x))))
       (let ((__function__ 'vl-range-revp))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (< (vl-range-msbidx x)
               (vl-range-lsbidx x))))
