(aig-not-list x) negates every AIG in the list x.

Function: aig-not-list

(defun aig-not-list (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (let ((__function__ 'aig-not-list))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (if (atom x)
                nil
                (cons (aig-not (car x))
                      (aig-not-list (cdr x))))))
