Simplify the definition of a given function, including the guard and measure.

See simplify-defun. Simplify-defun+ is merely a macro whose calls expand to corresponding calls of simplify-defun with :simplify-guard t and :simplify-measure t. For example:

ACL2 !>:trans1 (simplify-defun+ f1 :enable (foo bar))
 (SIMPLIFY-DEFUN F1
                 :SIMPLIFY-GUARD T
                 :SIMPLIFY-MEASURE T
                 :ENABLE (FOO BAR))
ACL2 !>:trans1 (show-simplify-defun+ f1 :enable (foo bar))
 (SHOW-SIMPLIFY-DEFUN F1
                      :SIMPLIFY-GUARD T
                      :SIMPLIFY-MEASURE T
                      :ENABLE (FOO BAR))
