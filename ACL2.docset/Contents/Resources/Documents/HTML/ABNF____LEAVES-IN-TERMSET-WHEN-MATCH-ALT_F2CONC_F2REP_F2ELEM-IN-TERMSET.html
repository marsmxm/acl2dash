Alternations, concatenation, repetitions, and elements whose terminal value notations all denote values in a set, can be matched only by (lists of (lists of)) trees whose terminal leaves are in the set.

The proof uses leaves-in-termset-when-match-num-val-in-termset and leaves-in-termset-when-match-char-val-in-termset.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: leaves-in-termset-when-match-alternation-in-termset

(defthm
 leaves-in-termset-when-match-alternation-in-termset
 (implies
  (and (tree-list-list-match-alternation-p treess alternation rules)
       (alternation-in-termset-p alternation termset)
       (rulelist-in-termset-p rules termset))
  (string-in-termset-p (tree-list-list->string treess)
                       termset)))
