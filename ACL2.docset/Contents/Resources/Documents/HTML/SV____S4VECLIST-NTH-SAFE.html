Like nth but, with proper fty-discipline for s4veclists. ``Safely'' causes a run-time error if n is out of bounds.

Function: s4veclist-nth-safe

(defun
 s4veclist-nth-safe (n x)
 (declare (xargs :guard (and (natp n) (s4veclist-p x))))
 (let
  ((__function__ 's4veclist-nth-safe))
  (declare (ignorable __function__))
  (mbe :logic (s4vec-fix (nth n x))
       :exec
       (or (nth n x)
           (raise "Index ~x0 too large for s4veclist of length ~x1."
                  n (len x))
           (s4vec-x)))))
