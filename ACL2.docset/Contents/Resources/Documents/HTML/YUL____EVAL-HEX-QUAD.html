Evaluate a quadruple of hex digits to a 16-bit unsigned integer.

Signature
(eval-hex-quad hq) → val
Arguments
hq — Guard (hex-quadp hq).
Returns
val — Type (acl2::ubyte16p val).

The digits are interpreted in big endian form.
