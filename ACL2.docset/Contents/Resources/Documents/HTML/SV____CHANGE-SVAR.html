Modifying constructor for svar structures.

Syntax
(change-svar x 
             [:name <name>] 
             [:delay <delay>] 
             [:nonblocking <nonblocking>] 
             [:override-test <override-test>] 
             [:override-val <override-val>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-svar.

We construct a new svar structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-svar

(defmacro
     change-svar (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'svar
                            x args
                            '((:name . svar->name)
                              (:delay . svar->delay)
                              (:nonblocking . svar->nonblocking)
                              (:override-test . svar->override-test)
                              (:override-val . svar->override-val))
                            'change-svar
                            'nil))
