Modifying constructor for vl-paramdecl structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-paramdecl x 
                     [:name <name>] 
                     [:localp <localp>] 
                     [:type <type>] 
                     [:atts <atts>] 
                     [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-paramdecl.

We construct a new vl-paramdecl structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-paramdecl

(defmacro change-vl-paramdecl (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-paramdecl
                                 x args
                                 '((:name . vl-paramdecl->name)
                                   (:localp . vl-paramdecl->localp)
                                   (:type . vl-paramdecl->type)
                                   (:atts . vl-paramdecl->atts)
                                   (:loc . vl-paramdecl->loc))
                                 'change-vl-paramdecl
                                 'remake-vl-paramdecl))
