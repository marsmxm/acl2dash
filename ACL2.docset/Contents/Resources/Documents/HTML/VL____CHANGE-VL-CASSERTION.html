Modifying constructor for vl-cassertion structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-cassertion x 
                      [:name <name>] 
                      [:type <type>] 
                      [:sequencep <sequencep>] 
                      [:condition <condition>] 
                      [:success <success>] 
                      [:failure <failure>] 
                      [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-cassertion.

We construct a new vl-cassertion structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-cassertion

(defmacro
    change-vl-cassertion (x &rest args)
    (std::change-aggregate 'vl-cassertion
                           x args
                           '((:name . vl-cassertion->name)
                             (:type . vl-cassertion->type)
                             (:sequencep . vl-cassertion->sequencep)
                             (:condition . vl-cassertion->condition)
                             (:success . vl-cassertion->success)
                             (:failure . vl-cassertion->failure)
                             (:loc . vl-cassertion->loc))
                           'change-vl-cassertion
                           'remake-vl-cassertion))
