Representation of any kind of part-select that is being applied to a some object in the design.

This is a sum-of-products (i.e., union) type, introduced by defflexsum.

Members
:none → vl-partselect-none
No part select.
:range → vl-partselect-range
A typical [msb:lsb] style part-select, e.g., [3:0] or [1:5].
:plusminus → vl-partselect-plusminus
An indexed part-select like [foo +: 3] or [bar -: 4].
