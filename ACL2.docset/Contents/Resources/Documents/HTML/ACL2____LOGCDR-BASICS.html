Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: logcdr-<-0

(defthm logcdr-<-0
        (equal (< (logcdr i) 0)
               (and (integerp i) (< i 0))))

Theorem: justify-logcdr-induction

(defthm justify-logcdr-induction
        (and (implies (> i 0) (< (logcdr i) i))
             (implies (< i -1) (< i (logcdr i)))))
