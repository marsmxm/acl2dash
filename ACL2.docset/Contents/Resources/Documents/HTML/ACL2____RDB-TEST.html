(rdb-test bsp i) is true iff the field of i specified by bsp is nonzero.

Signature
(rdb-test bsp i) → bool
Arguments
bsp — Guard (bspp bsp).
i — Guard (integerp i).

This is analogous to Common Lisp's (ldb-test bytespec integer).

(defun rdb-test (bsp i)
       (declare (xargs :guard (and (bspp bsp) (integerp i))))
       (let ((__function__ 'rdb-test))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (not (eql (rdb bsp i) 0))))
