(look-up-formals fn world) looks up the names of the arguments of the function fn, or causes a hard error if fn isn't a function.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: look-up-formals

(defun look-up-formals (fn world)
       (declare (xargs :guard (and (symbolp fn)
                                   (plist-worldp world))))
       (b* ((__function__ 'look-up-formals)
            (look (getprop fn 'acl2::formals
                           :bad 'acl2::current-acl2-world
                           world))
            ((when (eq look :bad))
             (raise "Can't look up the formals for ~x0!"
                    fn))
            ((unless (symbol-listp look))
             (raise "Expected a symbol-listp, but found ~x0!"
                    look)))
           look))
