Correctness theorems of the execution functions.

We locally enable the equivalence relations, since we always want to expand them in the proofs. Note that, if we put them into a goal hint, they would not apply to subgoals where computed hints apply; this is why we locally enable them instead.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: exec-expression-of-dead

(defthm exec-expression-of-dead
        (eoutcome-result-okeq
             (exec-expression expr cstate (funenv-dead funenv)
                              limit)
             (exec-expression expr cstate funenv limit)))
