Subtraction of two points of the twisted Edwards group.

Signature
(twisted-edwards-sub point1 point2 curve) → point
Arguments
point1 — Guard (pointp point1).
point2 — Guard (pointp point2).
curve — Guard (twisted-edwards-curvep curve).
Returns
point — Type (pointp point).

This is, as usual in groups, essentially an abbreviation for adding the first point to the negation of the second point.

Function: twisted-edwards-sub

(defun
 twisted-edwards-sub
 (point1 point2 curve)
 (declare (xargs :guard (and (pointp point1)
                             (pointp point2)
                             (twisted-edwards-curvep curve))))
 (declare
     (xargs :guard (and (twisted-edwards-curve-completep curve)
                        (point-on-twisted-edwards-p point1 curve)
                        (point-on-twisted-edwards-p point2 curve))))
 (let ((acl2::__function__ 'twisted-edwards-sub))
      (declare (ignorable acl2::__function__))
      (twisted-edwards-add point1
                           (twisted-edwards-neg point2 curve)
                           curve)))
