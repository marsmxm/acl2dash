(q-nor &rest args) constructs a UBDD representing the NOR of its arguments.

For instance:

(q-nor)         = t
(q-nor a)       = (q-not a)
(q-nor a b ...) = (q-not (q-or a b ...))

Macro: q-nor

(defmacro q-nor (&rest args)
          (cons 'q-not
                (cons (cons 'q-or args) 'nil)))
