Add a condition obligation hypothesis to a context.

Signature
(context-add-condition cond ctxt) → new-ctxt
Arguments
cond — Guard (expressionp cond).
ctxt — Guard (contextp ctxt).
Returns
new-ctxt — Type (contextp new-ctxt).

The hypothesis is added at the end, because its well-formedness may depend on the preceding hypotheses.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: context-add-condition

(defun
 context-add-condition (cond ctxt)
 (declare (xargs :guard (and (expressionp cond)
                             (contextp ctxt))))
 (let
  ((__function__ 'context-add-condition))
  (declare (ignorable __function__))
  (change-context ctxt
                  :obligation-hyps
                  (append (context->obligation-hyps ctxt)
                          (list (obligation-hyp-condition cond))))))
