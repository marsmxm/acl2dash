(4vs-and-lists x y) pairwise ands together the sexprs from two separate lists, forming a new sexpr list.

(defun 4vs-and-lists (x y)
       (declare (xargs :guard (equal (len x) (len y))))
       (if (atom x)
           nil
           (cons (4vs-and (car x) (car y))
                 (4vs-and-lists (cdr x) (cdr y)))))
