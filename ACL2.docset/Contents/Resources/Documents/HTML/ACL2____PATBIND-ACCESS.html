b* binder for accessing record structure fields introduced with ACL2's defrec.

This is a b* binder introduced with def-b*-binder.

Macro: patbind-access

(defmacro patbind-access (args forms rest-expr)
          (cons 'b*
                (cons (access-b*-bindings (car args)
                                          (car forms)
                                          (cdr args))
                      (cons rest-expr 'nil))))
