Definition

This is an ordinary honsing constructor introduced by defaggregate.

Function: honsed-propagate-limits

(defun
  honsed-propagate-limits (max-ops)
  (declare (xargs :guard (and (maybe-natp max-ops))))
  (mbe :logic (propagate-limits max-ops)
       :exec
       (hons :propagate-limits (hons (hons 'max-ops max-ops) nil))))
