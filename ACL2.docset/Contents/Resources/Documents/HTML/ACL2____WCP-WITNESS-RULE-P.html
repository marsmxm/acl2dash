(wcp-witness-rule-p x) is a std::defaggregate of the following fields.

  • name — Invariant (symbolp name).
  • enabledp
  • term — Invariant (pseudo-termp term).
  • expr — Invariant (pseudo-termp expr).
  • restriction
  • theorem — Invariant (symbolp theorem).
  • generalize — Invariant (and (alistp generalize) (pseudo-term-listp (strip-cars generalize)) (symbol-listp (strip-cdrs generalize))) .
