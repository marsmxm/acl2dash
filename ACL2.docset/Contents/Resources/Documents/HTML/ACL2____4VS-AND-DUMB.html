(4vs-and-dumb a b) constructs exactly the 4v-sexpr (and a b).

This is dumb in that it doesn't do the simple optimizations of 4vs-and, but it has a nicer 4v-sexpr-vars theorem because of this.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: 4vs-and-dumb

(defun 4vs-and-dumb (a b)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (hons-list 'and a b))

Theorem: 4v-sexpr-eval-of-4vs-and-dumb

(defthm 4v-sexpr-eval-of-4vs-and-dumb
        (equal (4v-sexpr-eval (4vs-and-dumb a b) env)
               (4v-and (4v-sexpr-eval a env)
                       (4v-sexpr-eval b env))))

Theorem: 4v-sexpr-vars-of-4vs-and-dumb

        (equal (4v-sexpr-vars (4vs-and-dumb a b))
               (hons-alphorder-merge (4v-sexpr-vars a)
