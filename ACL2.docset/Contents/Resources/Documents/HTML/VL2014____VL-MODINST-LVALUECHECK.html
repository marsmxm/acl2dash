Check well-formedness of lvalues in a vl-modinst-p.

Signature
(vl-modinst-lvaluecheck x warnings) → new-warnings
Arguments
x — Guard (vl-modinst-p x).
warnings — Ordinary warnings accumulator.
    Guard (vl-warninglist-p warnings).
Returns
new-warnings — Type (vl-warninglist-p new-warnings).

We check the lvalues throughout x for well-formedness in the sense of vl-expr-lvaluep, and generate non-fatal warnings for any problematic lvalues encountered.

Function: vl-modinst-lvaluecheck

(defun vl-modinst-lvaluecheck (x warnings)
       (declare (xargs :guard (and (vl-modinst-p x)
                                   (vl-warninglist-p warnings))))
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (let ((__function__ 'vl-modinst-lvaluecheck))
            (declare (ignorable __function__))
            (vl-arguments-lvaluecheck (vl-modinst->portargs x)
                                      (vl-modinst->loc x)
                                      (vl-modinst->instname x)
                                      warnings)))
