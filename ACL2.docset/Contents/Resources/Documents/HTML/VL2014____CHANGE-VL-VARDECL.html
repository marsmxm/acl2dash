Modifying constructor for vl-vardecl structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-vardecl x 
                   [:name <name>] 
                   [:type <type>] 
                   [:nettype <nettype>] 
                   [:constp <constp>] 
                   [:varp <varp>] 
                   [:lifetime <lifetime>] 
                   [:initval <initval>] 
                   [:vectoredp <vectoredp>] 
                   [:scalaredp <scalaredp>] 
                   [:delay <delay>] 
                   [:cstrength <cstrength>] 
                   [:atts <atts>] 
                   [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-vardecl.

We construct a new vl-vardecl structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-vardecl

(defmacro
     change-vl-vardecl (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'vl-vardecl
                            x args
                            '((:name . vl-vardecl->name)
                              (:type . vl-vardecl->type)
                              (:nettype . vl-vardecl->nettype)
                              (:constp . vl-vardecl->constp)
                              (:varp . vl-vardecl->varp)
                              (:lifetime . vl-vardecl->lifetime)
                              (:initval . vl-vardecl->initval)
                              (:vectoredp . vl-vardecl->vectoredp)
                              (:scalaredp . vl-vardecl->scalaredp)
                              (:delay . vl-vardecl->delay)
                              (:cstrength . vl-vardecl->cstrength)
                              (:atts . vl-vardecl->atts)
                              (:loc . vl-vardecl->loc))
                            'change-vl-vardecl
                            'remake-vl-vardecl))
