Signature

This lifts the restriction captured by tree-*cwsp-cnl-restriction-p from (*c-wsp c-nl) to ( rule / (*c-wsp c-nl) ). If the alternative is rule, no restriction applies; otherwise, tree-*cwsp-cnl-restriction-p applies.
