(4vs-and-list-dumb sexprs) reduction ands together all the sexprs in the list x, producing a single sexpr representing their conjunction.

This is dumb in that it doesn't do the simple optimizations of 4vs-and-list, but it has a nicer 4v-sexpr-vars theorem because of this.

Function: 4vs-and-list-dumb

(defun 4vs-and-list-dumb (sexprs)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (if (atom sexprs)
           *4vt-sexpr*
           (4vs-and-dumb (car sexprs)
                         (4vs-and-list-dumb (cdr sexprs)))))
