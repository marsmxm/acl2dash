The accept_on, reject_on, sync_accept_on, and sync_reject_on operators.

These are a bit unusual and take both a condition and a property as their arguments.

This is an ordinary defenum.

Function: vl-property-acceptop-p

(defun vl-property-acceptop-p (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (or (eq x ':vl-prop-accepton)
           (eq x ':vl-prop-syncaccepton)
           (eq x ':vl-prop-rejecton)
           (eq x ':vl-prop-syncrejecton)))
