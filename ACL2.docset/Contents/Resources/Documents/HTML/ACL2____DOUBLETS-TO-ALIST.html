Turn a true list of doublets (i.e. lists of length 2) into the corresponding alist.

This is the inverse of alist-to-doublets.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: doublets-to-alist

(defun doublets-to-alist (doublets)
       (declare (xargs :guard (doublet-listp doublets)))
       (if (endp doublets)
           nil
           (cons (cons (car (first doublets))
                       (cadr (first doublets)))
                 (doublets-to-alist (rest doublets)))))

Theorem: doublets-to-alist-of-alist-to-doublets

(defthm doublets-to-alist-of-alist-to-doublets
        (implies (alistp x)
                 (equal (doublets-to-alist (alist-to-doublets x))
                        x)))
