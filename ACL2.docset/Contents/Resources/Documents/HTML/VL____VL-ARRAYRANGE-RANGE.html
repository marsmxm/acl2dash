A with [msb:lsb] stream expression part.

This is a product type, introduced by defflexsum in support of vl-arrayrange.

Fields
range vl-range
The whole range, e.g., [20:16], as an atomic vl-range.

Note that the b* binder sets up extra bindings for .msb and .lsb, so you can typically access the guts of the interior range directly.
