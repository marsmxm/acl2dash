Representation of an array range for use in with operators in streaming packing/unpacking expressions.

This is a sum-of-products (i.e., union) type, introduced by defflexsum.

Members
:none → vl-arrayrange-none
Used for plain stream expressions with no with part.
:range → vl-arrayrange-range
A with [msb:lsb] stream expression part.
:plusminus → vl-arrayrange-plusminus
A with [base +: width] or with [base -: width] stream expression part.
:index → vl-arrayrange-index
A with index stream expression part.
