Theorems about add-var and add-vars for the static soundness proof.

We prove two theorems to rephrase add-var and add-vars as insert and set::list-insert. The first is used to prove the second, which is used in some other theorem (find it in hints).

We have two variants of add-vars applied to append that differ only in the exact hypotheses. This seems unfortunate, so we will try and consolidate them. We also have a theorem about errors for add-vars of append.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: add-var-to-insert

(defthm add-var-to-insert
        (b* ((vartab1 (add-var var vartab)))
            (implies (not (resulterrp vartab1))
                     (equal vartab1
                            (insert (identifier-fix var)
                                    (vartable-fix vartab))))))

Theorem: add-vars-to-list-insert

(defthm
    add-vars-to-list-insert
    (b* ((vartab1 (add-vars vars vartab)))
        (implies (not (resulterrp vartab1))
                 (equal vartab1
                        (set::list-insert (identifier-list-fix vars)
                                          (vartable-fix vartab))))))

Theorem: add-vars-of-append

(defthm
 add-vars-of-append
 (implies
   (and (not (resulterrp (add-vars vars1 vartab)))
        (not (resulterrp (add-vars vars2 (add-vars vars1 vartab)))))
   (equal (add-vars (append vars1 vars2) vartab)
          (add-vars vars2 (add-vars vars1 vartab)))))

Theorem: add-vars-of-append-2

(defthm
  add-vars-of-append-2
  (implies (not (resulterrp (add-vars (append vars1 vars2) vartab)))
           (equal (add-vars (append vars1 vars2) vartab)
                  (add-vars vars2 (add-vars vars1 vartab)))))

Theorem: resulterrp-of-add-vars-of-append

(defthm
     resulterrp-of-add-vars-of-append
     (implies (resulterrp (add-vars vars vartab))
              (resulterrp (add-vars (append vars vars1) vartab))))
