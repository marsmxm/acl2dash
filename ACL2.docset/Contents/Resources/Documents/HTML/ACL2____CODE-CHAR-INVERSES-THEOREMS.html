code-char and char-code are mutual inverses.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: char-code-of-code-char

(defthm char-code-of-code-char
        (equal (char-code (code-char code))
               (unsigned-byte-fix 8 code)))

Theorem: code-char-of-char-code

(defthm code-char-of-char-code
        (equal (code-char (char-code char))
               (char-fix char)))
