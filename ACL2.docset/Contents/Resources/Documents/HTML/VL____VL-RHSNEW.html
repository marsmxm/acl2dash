A 'new' invocation being used as a right-hand-side.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-rhs.

Fields
arrsize — vl-maybe-expr
For new arrays, this is the dimension of the array. For instance, in arr[0] = new [4] this would be the 4. For ordinary new instances of classes, this is just nil.
args — vl-exprlist
Arguments to the new class or array.
