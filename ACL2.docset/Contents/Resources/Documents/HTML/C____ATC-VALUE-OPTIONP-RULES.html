Rules for discharging value-optionp hypotheses.

Some symbolic execution rules have hypotheses saying that certain terms are optional values, i.e. satisfy value-optionp. These are discharged by the rules here. The executable counterpart of value-optionp takes care of the nil case. The non-nil case is taken care by backchaining to the rules in atc-valuep-rules.
