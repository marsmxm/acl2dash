Modifying constructor for vl-genarray structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-genarray x 
                    [:name <name>] 
                    [:var <var>] 
                    [:blocks <blocks>] 
                    [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-genarray.

We construct a new vl-genarray structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-genarray

(defmacro change-vl-genarray (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-genarray
                                 x args
                                 '((:name . vl-genarray->name)
                                   (:var . vl-genarray->var)
                                   (:blocks . vl-genarray->blocks)
                                   (:loc . vl-genarray->loc))
                                 'change-vl-genarray
                                 'remake-vl-genarray))
