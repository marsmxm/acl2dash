Modifying constructor for vl-usertype structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-usertype x 
                    [:name <name>] 
                    [:res <res>] 
                    [:pdims <pdims>] 
                    [:udims <udims>] 
                    [:virtual-intfc <virtual-intfc>] 
                    [:intfc-params <intfc-params>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-usertype.

We construct a new vl-usertype structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-usertype

(defmacro change-vl-usertype (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'vl-usertype
               x args
               '((:name . vl-usertype->name)
                 (:res . vl-usertype->res)
                 (:pdims . vl-usertype->pdims)
                 (:udims . vl-usertype->udims)
                 (:virtual-intfc . vl-usertype->virtual-intfc)
                 (:intfc-params . vl-usertype->intfc-params))
               'change-vl-usertype
               'remake-vl-usertype))
