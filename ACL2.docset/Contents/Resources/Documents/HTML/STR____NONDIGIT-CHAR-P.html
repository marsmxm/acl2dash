Recognize characters that are not decimal digits.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: nondigit-char-p

(defun nondigit-char-p (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (and (characterp x)
            (b* ((code (char-code x)))
                (not (and (<= (char-code #\0) code)
                          (<= code (char-code #\9)))))))
