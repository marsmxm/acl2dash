Modifying constructor for vl-nonatom structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-nonatom x 
                   [:op <op>] 
                   [:atts <atts>] 
                   [:args <args>] 
                   [:finalwidth <finalwidth>] 
                   [:finaltype <finaltype>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-nonatom.

We construct a new vl-nonatom structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-nonatom

(defmacro
     change-vl-nonatom (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'vl-nonatom
                            x args
                            '((:op . vl-nonatom->op)
                              (:atts . vl-nonatom->atts)
                              (:args . vl-nonatom->args)
                              (:finalwidth . vl-nonatom->finalwidth)
                              (:finaltype . vl-nonatom->finaltype))
                            'change-vl-nonatom
                            'remake-vl-nonatom))
