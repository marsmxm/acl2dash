(fn-info-elt-p x) is a std::defaggregate of the following fields.

  • source-fn — Invariant (symbolp source-fn).
  • target-fn — Invariant (symbolp target-fn).
  • iso-thm — Invariant (symbolp iso-thm).
  • osi-thm — Invariant (symbolp osi-thm).
  • arg-types — Invariant (symbol-listp arg-types).
  • result-types — Invariant (symbol-listp result-types).
