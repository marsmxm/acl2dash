b* binder for invoking with-stolen-alists for the remainder of a b* form.

This is a b* binder introduced with def-b*-binder.

Macro: patbind-with-stolen

(defmacro patbind-with-stolen
          (args forms rest-expr)
          (declare (xargs :guard (not forms))
                   (ignorable forms))
          (cons 'with-stolen-alists
                (cons args (cons rest-expr 'nil))))
