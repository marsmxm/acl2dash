A streaming concatenation (pack or unpack) operation, e.g., {<< 16 {a,b,c}}.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-expr.

Fields
dir — vl-leftright-p
The kind of stream operator: :left for << or :right for >>.
size — vl-slicesize
The slice size or an indication that there is no slice size. For instance, the 16 in {<< 16 {a,b,c}}.
parts — vl-streamexprlist
The stream_expressions that make up the stream_concatenation, i.e., a, b, and c for {<< 16 {a,b,c}}. These aren't just ordinary expressions since they can have with clauses.
atts — vl-atts
Any (* foo = bar, baz *) style attributes.
