Modifying constructor for flatten-res structures.

Syntax
(change-flatten-res x 
                    [:assigns <assigns>] 
                    [:fixups <fixups>] 
                    [:constraints <constraints>] 
                    [:var-decl-map <var-decl-map>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-flatten-res.

We construct a new flatten-res structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-flatten-res

(defmacro change-flatten-res (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'flatten-res
               x args
               '((:assigns . flatten-res->assigns)
                 (:fixups . flatten-res->fixups)
                 (:constraints . flatten-res->constraints)
                 (:var-decl-map . flatten-res->var-decl-map))
               'change-flatten-res
               'nil))
