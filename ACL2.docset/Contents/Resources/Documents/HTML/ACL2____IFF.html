Logical ``if and only if''

Iff is the ACL2 biconditional, ``if and only if''. (iff P Q) means that either P and Q are both false (i.e., nil) or both true (i.e., not nil).

Function: iff

(defun iff (p q)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (if p (if q t nil) (if q nil t)))
