Modifying constructor for vl-alias structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-alias x 
                 [:lhs <lhs>] 
                 [:rhs <rhs>] 
                 [:atts <atts>] 
                 [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-alias.

We construct a new vl-alias structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-alias

(defmacro change-vl-alias (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-alias
                                 x args
                                 '((:lhs . vl-alias->lhs)
                                   (:rhs . vl-alias->rhs)
                                   (:atts . vl-alias->atts)
                                   (:loc . vl-alias->loc))
                                 'change-vl-alias
                                 'remake-vl-alias))
