This is our preferred way to construct glcp-config-p structures. It simply conses together a structure with the specified fields.

This macro generates a new glcp-config-p structure from scratch. See also change-glcp-config, which can "change" an existing structure, instead.

The glcp-config-p structures we create here are just constructed with ordinary cons. If you want to create honsed structures, see make-honsed-glcp-config instead.

Definition

This is an ordinary make- macro introduced by std::defaggregate.

Macro: make-glcp-config

(defmacro make-glcp-config (&rest args)
          (std::make-aggregate
               'glcp-config
               args
               '((:abort-indeterminate . t)
                 (:abort-ctrex . t)
                 (:exec-ctrex . t)
                 (:ctrex-transform quote (lambda (x) x))
                 (:abort-vacuous . t)
                 (:check-vacuous . t)
                 (:n-counterexamples . 3)
                 (:hyp-clk . 1000000)
                 (:concl-clk . 1000000)
                 (:clause-proc)
                 (:overrides)
                 (:param-bfr . t)
                 (:term-level-counterexample-scheme . :depgraph)
                 (:top-level-term)
                 (:shape-spec-alist)
                 (:run-before-cases)
                 (:run-after-cases)
                 (:case-split-override)
                 (:split-conses)
                 (:split-fncalls)
                 (:lift-ifsp . t)
                 (:prof-enabledp)
