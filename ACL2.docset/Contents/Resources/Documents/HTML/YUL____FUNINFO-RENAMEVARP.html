Variable renaming relation over function information.

Signature
(funinfo-renamevarp old new) → yes/no
Arguments
old — Guard (funinfop old).
new — Guard (funinfop new).
Returns
yes/no — Type (booleanp yes/no).

An old and new function information are related by variable renaming when their inputs and outputs form a renaming under which the bodies are related by the renaming relation.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: funinfo-renamevarp

(defun
     funinfo-renamevarp (old new)
     (declare (xargs :guard (and (funinfop old) (funinfop new))))
     (let ((__function__ 'funinfo-renamevarp))
          (declare (ignorable __function__))
          (b* ((ren (add-vars-to-var-renaming (funinfo->inputs old)
                                              (funinfo->inputs new)
                                              (renaming nil)))
               ((when (resulterrp ren)) nil)
               (ren (add-vars-to-var-renaming (funinfo->outputs old)
                                              (funinfo->outputs new)
                                              ren))
               ((when (resulterrp ren)) nil))
              (not (resulterrp (block-renamevar (funinfo->body old)
                                                (funinfo->body new)
                                                ren))))))
