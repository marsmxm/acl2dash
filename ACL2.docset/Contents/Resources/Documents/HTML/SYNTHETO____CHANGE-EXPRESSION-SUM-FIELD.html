Modifying constructor for expression-sum-field structures.

Syntax
(change-expression-sum-field x 
                             [:type <type>] 
                             [:target <target>] 
                             [:alternative <alternative>] 
                             [:field <field>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-expression-sum-field.

We construct a new expression-sum-field structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by fty::defprod.

Macro: change-expression-sum-field

(defmacro change-expression-sum-field
          (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'expression-sum-field
               x args
               '((:type . expression-sum-field->type)
                 (:target . expression-sum-field->target)
                 (:alternative . expression-sum-field->alternative)
                 (:field . expression-sum-field->field))
               'change-expression-sum-field
               'nil))
