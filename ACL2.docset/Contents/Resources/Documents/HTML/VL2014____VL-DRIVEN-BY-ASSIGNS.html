(vl-driven-by-assigns x) applies vl-driven-by-assign to every member of the list x, and appends together all the resulting lists.

This is an ordinary defmapappend.

Function: vl-driven-by-assigns

(defun vl-driven-by-assigns (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard (vl-assignlist-p x)))
       (mbe :logic (if (consp x)
                       (append (vl-driven-by-assign (car x))
                               (vl-driven-by-assigns (cdr x)))
                       nil)
            :exec (reverse (vl-driven-by-assigns-exec x nil))))
