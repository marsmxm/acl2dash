Modifying constructor for obs-constprop-config structures.

Syntax
(change-obs-constprop-config x 
                             [:gatesimp <gatesimp>] 
                             [:constprop-iterations <constprop-iterations>] 
                             [:obs-hyp-max-size <obs-hyp-max-size>] 
                             [:obs-concl-min-size <obs-concl-min-size>] 
                             [:obs-min-ratio <obs-min-ratio>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-obs-constprop-config.

We construct a new obs-constprop-config structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by fty::defprod.

Macro: change-obs-constprop-config

(defmacro
 change-obs-constprop-config
 (x &rest args)
 (std::change-aggregate
    'obs-constprop-config
    x args
    '((:gatesimp . obs-constprop-config->gatesimp)
      (:constprop-iterations
           .
           obs-constprop-config->constprop-iterations)
      (:obs-hyp-max-size .
                         obs-constprop-config->obs-hyp-max-size)
      (:obs-concl-min-size .
                           obs-constprop-config->obs-concl-min-size)
      (:obs-min-ratio . obs-constprop-config->obs-min-ratio))
    'change-obs-constprop-config
    'nil))
