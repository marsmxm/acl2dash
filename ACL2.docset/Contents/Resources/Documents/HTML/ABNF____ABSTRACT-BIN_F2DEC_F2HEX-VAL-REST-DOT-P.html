Discriminate between a ("." 1*BIT) and a ("-" 1*BIT) parse tree, or between a ("." 1*DIGIT) and a ("-" 1*DIGIT) parse tree, or between a ("." 1*HEXDIG) and a ("-" 1*HEXDIG) parse tree.

Signature
(abstract-bin/dec/hex-val-rest-dot-p tree) → yes/no
Arguments
tree — Guard (treep tree).
Returns
yes/no — Type (booleanp yes/no).

This is used by abstract-bin-val-rest, abstract-dec-val-rest, and abstract-hex-val-rest to distinguish between the two alternatives in the definiens of bin-val, dec-val, and hex-val. For the first alternative, t is returned; for the second alternative, nil is returned.

       (tree)
