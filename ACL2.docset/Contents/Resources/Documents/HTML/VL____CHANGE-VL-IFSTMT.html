Modifying constructor for vl-ifstmt structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-ifstmt x 
                  [:condition <condition>] 
                  [:truebranch <truebranch>] 
                  [:falsebranch <falsebranch>] 
                  [:atts <atts>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-ifstmt.

We construct a new vl-ifstmt structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-ifstmt

(defmacro
    change-vl-ifstmt (x &rest args)
    (std::change-aggregate 'vl-ifstmt
                           x args
                           '((:condition . vl-ifstmt->condition)
                             (:truebranch . vl-ifstmt->truebranch)
                             (:falsebranch . vl-ifstmt->falsebranch)
                             (:atts . vl-ifstmt->atts))
                           'change-vl-ifstmt
                           'remake-vl-ifstmt))
