Logical bit fix. (lbfix b) is logically identical to (bfix b) but executes as the identity. It requires (bitp b) as a guard, and expands to just b.

Macro: lbfix

(defmacro lbfix (x)
          (cons 'mbe
                (cons ':logic
                      (cons (cons 'bfix (cons x 'nil))
                            (cons ':exec (cons x 'nil))))))
