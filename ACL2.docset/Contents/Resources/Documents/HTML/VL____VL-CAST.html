A casting expression, e.g., int'(2.0 * 3.0).

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-expr.

Fields
to — vl-casttype
The new type/size/signedness/constness to cast the argument to, e.g., int above.
expr — vl-expr
The expression being cast to something else, e.g., 2.0 * 3.0 above.
atts — vl-atts
Any (* foo = bar, baz *) style attributes.
