(combine16u a1 a0) merges unsigned bytes, producing the 16-bit unsigned interpretation of (a1 << 8) | a0.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: combine16u$inline

(defun combine16u$inline (a1 a0)
       (declare (type (unsigned-byte 8) a1 a0))
       (mbe :logic (logior (ash (nfix a1) 8) (nfix a0))
            :exec (the (unsigned-byte 16)
                       (logior (ash a1 8) a0))))

Theorem: combine16u-unsigned-byte

(defthm combine16u-unsigned-byte
        (implies (and (force (unsigned-byte-p 8 a1))
                      (force (unsigned-byte-p 8 a0)))
                 (unsigned-byte-p 16 (combine16u a1 a0))))

Theorem: combine16u-loghead-logtail

(defthm combine16u-loghead-logtail
        (implies (natp i)
                 (equal (combine16u (logtail 8 i) (loghead 8 i))
                        i)))
