Modifying constructor for vl-assignstmt structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-assignstmt x 
                      [:type <type>] 
                      [:lvalue <lvalue>] 
                      [:rhs <rhs>] 
                      [:loc <loc>] 
                      [:ctrl <ctrl>] 
                      [:atts <atts>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-assignstmt.

We construct a new vl-assignstmt structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-assignstmt

(defmacro change-vl-assignstmt (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-assignstmt
                                 x args
                                 '((:type . vl-assignstmt->type)
                                   (:lvalue . vl-assignstmt->lvalue)
                                   (:rhs . vl-assignstmt->rhs)
                                   (:loc . vl-assignstmt->loc)
                                   (:ctrl . vl-assignstmt->ctrl)
                                   (:atts . vl-assignstmt->atts))
                                 'change-vl-assignstmt
                                 'remake-vl-assignstmt))
