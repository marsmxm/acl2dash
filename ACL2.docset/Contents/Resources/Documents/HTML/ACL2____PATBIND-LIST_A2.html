b* binder for list* decomposition using car/cdr.

Usage:

(b* (((list* a b c) lst)) form)

is equivalent to

(b* ((a (car lst))
     (tmp1 (cdr lst))
     (b (car tmp1))
     (c (cdr tmp1)))
  form)

Each of the arguments to the list* binder may be a recursive binder, and list* may be nested inside other bindings.
