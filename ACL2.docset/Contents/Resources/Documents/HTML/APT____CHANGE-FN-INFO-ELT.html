A copying macro that lets you create new fn-info-elt-p structures, based on existing structures.

Syntax:

(change-fn-info-elt x 
                    [:source-fn <source-fn>] 
                    [:target-fn <target-fn>] 
                    [:iso-thm <iso-thm>] 
                    [:osi-thm <osi-thm>] 
                    [:arg-types <arg-types>] 
                    [:result-types <result-types>])

This is a sometimes useful alternative to make-fn-info-elt. It constructs a new fn-info-elt-p structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by std::defaggregate.

Macro: change-fn-info-elt

(defmacro change-fn-info-elt (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate
               'fn-info-elt
               x args
               '((:source-fn . fn-info-elt->source-fn)
                 (:target-fn . fn-info-elt->target-fn)
                 (:iso-thm . fn-info-elt->iso-thm)
                 (:osi-thm . fn-info-elt->osi-thm)
                 (:arg-types . fn-info-elt->arg-types)
                 (:result-types . fn-info-elt->result-types))
               'change-fn-info-elt
               'nil))
