Recognizer for integers and nil.

This is like an option type; when x satisfies maybe-integerp, then either it is a integer or nothing.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: maybe-integerp$inline

(defun maybe-integerp$inline (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (or (not x) (integerp x)))

Theorem: maybe-integerp-compound-recognizer

(defthm maybe-integerp-compound-recognizer
        (equal (maybe-integerp x)
               (or (integerp x) (not x)))
        :rule-classes :compound-recognizer)
