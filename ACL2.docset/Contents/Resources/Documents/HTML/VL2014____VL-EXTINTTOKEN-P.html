Tokens for unsized, unbased SystemVerilog integers, e.g., '0, '1, 'x, and 'z.

(vl-extinttoken-p x) is a defaggregate of the following fields.

  • etext — The characters that gave rise to this token.
        Invariant (and (vl-echarlist-p etext) (consp etext) (true-listp etext)).
  • value — The kind of extended integer this is.
        Invariant (vl-bit-p value).
