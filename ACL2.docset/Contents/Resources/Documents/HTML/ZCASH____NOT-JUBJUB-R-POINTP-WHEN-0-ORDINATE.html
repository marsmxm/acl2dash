A Jubjub point with 0 ordinate is not in jubjub-r-pointp.

This indirectly depends on the associativity of twisted Edwards addition, so we add that as a hypothesis (see ecurve::twisted-edwards-add-associativity).

From the curve equation and the fact that the ordinate is 0, it turns out that doubling the point yields point-0-m1. Thus multiplying the point by 4 yields the zero point, and therefore the point does not have order jubjub-r. The point itself is not the zero point, which is (0,1).

This is part of Theorem 5.4.7 in [ZPS].

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: not-jubjub-r-pointp-when-0-ordinate

(defthm not-jubjub-r-pointp-when-0-ordinate
        (implies (and (ecurve::twisted-edwards-add-associativity)
                      (jubjub-pointp point)
                      (equal (jubjub-point->v point) 0))
                 (not (jubjub-r-pointp point)))
        :rule-classes :rewrite)
