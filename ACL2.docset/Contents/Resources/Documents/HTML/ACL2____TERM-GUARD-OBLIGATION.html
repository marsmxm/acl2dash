Formula expressing the guard obligation of a term.

Signature
(term-guard-obligation term state) → obligation
Arguments
term — Guard (pseudo-termp term).
Returns
obligation — A pseudo-termp.

The case in which term is a symbol is dealt with separately because guard-obligation interprets a symbol as a function or theorem name, not as a variable.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: term-guard-obligation

(defun
 term-guard-obligation (term state)
 (declare (xargs :stobjs (state)))
 (declare (xargs :guard (pseudo-termp term)))
 (let
  ((__function__ 'term-guard-obligation))
  (declare (ignorable __function__))
  (b*
    (((when (symbolp term)) *t*)
     ((mv erp val)
      (guard-obligation term nil nil t __function__ state))
     ((when erp)
      (raise "Error ~x0 when computing the guard obligation of ~x1."
             erp term))
     (obligation-clauses (cadr val))
     (obligation-formula (termify-clause-set obligation-clauses)))
    obligation-formula)))
