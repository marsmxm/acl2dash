Fixtype of computation states.

This is a product type introduced by fty::defprod.

Fields
frames — frame-list
heap — heap

A computation state consists of a stack of frames and a heap. More components may be added as our modeling coverage of C increases.

The stack grows leftward and shrinks rightward, i.e. push is cons, pop is cdr, and top is car.
