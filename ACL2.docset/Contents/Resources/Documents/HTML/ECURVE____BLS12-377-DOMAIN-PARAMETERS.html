Domain parameters of the BLS12-377 elliptic curve.

The BLS12-377 elliptic curve is called E_{BLS} in Zexe: Enabling Decentralized Private Computation.

Here we introduce some of the parameters of this and of related elliptic curves, as described in Section 7 and in Figure 16 of that paper.
