Equivalence of faig constants (faig-const-p).

Two objects are faig-const-equiv if their faig-const-fixes are equal.

Function: faig-const-equiv

(defun faig-const-equiv (x y)
       (and (equal (faig-const-fix x)
                   (faig-const-fix y))))
