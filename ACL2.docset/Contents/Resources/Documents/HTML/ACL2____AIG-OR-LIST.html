(aig-or-list x) ors together all of the AIGs in the list x.

Signature
(aig-or-list x) â†’ aig

As a dumb attempt at optimization, we try to avoid consing if we see that there's a t anywhere in the list. This won't win very often, but it is quite cheap and it can win big when it does win by avoiding a lot of AIG construction.
