Check if two expressions are related by variable renaming.

Signature
(expression-renamevar old new ren) → _
Arguments
old — Guard (expressionp old).
new — Guard (expressionp new).
ren — Guard (renamingp ren).
Returns
_ — Type (resulterr-optionp _).

Old and new expressions must be of the same kind, and have constituents recursively related. Literals must be identical, since they have no variables.
