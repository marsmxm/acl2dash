Definitions and Theorems

Function: svtv->out-width

(defun
     svtv->out-width (name svtv)
     (declare (xargs :guard (svtv-p svtv)))
     (let ((__function__ 'svtv->out-width))
          (declare (ignorable __function__))
          (b* ((look (hons-assoc-equal name (svtv->outmasks svtv)))
               ((unless look)
                (raise "Unknown output: ~x0~%" name)
                0))
              (integer-length (nfix (cdr look))))))
