Representation of a (possibly) hierarchical reference to something in the design. For example: cat.dog[3][2][1].elf.

This is a sum-of-products (i.e., union) type, introduced by defflexsum.

Members
:end → vl-hidexpr-end
A lone identifier, or the final part of a hierarchical identifier.
:dot → vl-hidexpr-dot
A single dot operation, perhaps with associated indices, that connects parts of a hierarchical identifier.
