If suppress is non-nil, wrap the event-form form with a construct to suppress its output. Otherwise, just return form.

Function: maybe-suppress-output

(defun maybe-suppress-output (suppress form)
       (declare (xargs :guard (pseudo-event-formp form)))
       (let ((__function__ 'maybe-suppress-output))

            (if suppress (suppress-output form)
