Executable attachment for the PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA-512 interface.

We define a wrapper of the executable definition and attach the wrapper to the constrained function. The wrapper just serves to use the fixtypes for byte lists, to fix the password and salt input accordingly, and to fix the iterations and length inputs to positive integers.

Definitions and Theorems

Function: pbkdf2-hmac-sha-512-wrapper

(defun
 pbkdf2-hmac-sha-512-wrapper
 (password salt iterations length)
 (declare (xargs :guard (and (byte-listp password)
                             (byte-listp salt)
                             (posp iterations)
                             (posp length))))
 (declare (xargs :guard (and (< (len password) (expt 2 125))
                             (< (len salt) (- (expt 2 125) 132))
                             (<= length (* (1- (expt 2 32)) 64)))))
 (let
   ((__function__ 'pbkdf2-hmac-sha-512-wrapper))
   (declare (ignorable __function__))
   (b* ((password (mbe :logic (byte-list-fix password)
                       :exec password))
        (salt (mbe :logic (byte-list-fix salt)
                   :exec salt))
        (iterations (mbe :logic (pos-fix iterations)
                         :exec iterations))
        (length (mbe :logic (pos-fix length)
                     :exec length)))
       (kdf::pbkdf2-hmac-sha-512 password salt iterations length))))
