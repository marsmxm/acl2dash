A sequence or property expression with a clocking event.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-propexpr.

Fields
trigger — vl-evatomlist
A clocking_event, e.g., the posedge foo part of a sequence expression like @(posedge foo) ready ##1 qvalid.
then — vl-propexpr
The sequence to match when this clocking event occurs, e.g., the ready ##1 qvalid part of the above sequence.
