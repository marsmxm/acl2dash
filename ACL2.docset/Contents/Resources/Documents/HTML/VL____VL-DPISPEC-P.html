Representation of the "DPI" or "DPI-C" specification used in DPI import/exports.

This governs low level arcana like how packed array arguments are passed to C.

This is an ordinary defenum.

Function: vl-dpispec-p

(defun vl-dpispec-p (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard t))
       (or (eq x ':vl-dpi) (eq x ':vl-dpi-c)))
