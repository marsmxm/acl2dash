Macro to prove several theorems about behavior of conditional overrides on an SVTV.

Usage:

(def-svtv-override-thms <svtv-name> <export-name>)

Prerequisite: The SVTV must be defined with defsvtv$ or defsvtv$-phasewise (or otherwise result from populating a svtv-data stobj), and the contents of the stobj thus populated must be exported into a regular object <export-name> using def-svtv-data-export. Additionally, the setting for the monotonify argument of the defsvtv$ form must have been t (the default). The export object may be defined locally.

This simply combines the def-svtv-partial-monotonic, def-svtv-overrides-crux, and def-svtv-overrides-correct events. See def-svtv-overrides-correct for the highest-level theorem that is proved.
