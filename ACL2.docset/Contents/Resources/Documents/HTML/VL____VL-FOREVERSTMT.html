Representation of forever statements.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-stmt.

Fields
body — vl-stmt
atts — vl-atts
Any (* foo, bar = 1*) style attributes associated with this statement.

General syntax of a forever statement:

forever <body>;

See Section 9.6 (page 130). The forever statement continuously executes body.
