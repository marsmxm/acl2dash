Representation of an if/then/else statement.

This is a product type, introduced by deftagsum in support of vl-stmt.

Fields
condition — vl-expr
truebranch — vl-stmt
falsebranch — vl-stmt
atts — vl-atts
Any (* foo, bar = 1*) style attributes associated with this statement.

General Form:

if (<condition>)
   <truebranch>
else
   <falsebranch>
