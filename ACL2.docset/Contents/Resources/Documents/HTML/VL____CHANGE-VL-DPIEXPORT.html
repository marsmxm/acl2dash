Modifying constructor for vl-dpiexport structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-dpiexport x 
                     [:name <name>] 
                     [:c-name <c-name>] 
                     [:spec <spec>] 
                     [:fntask <fntask>] 
                     [:atts <atts>] 
                     [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-dpiexport.

We construct a new vl-dpiexport structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-dpiexport

(defmacro change-vl-dpiexport (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-dpiexport
                                 x args
                                 '((:name . vl-dpiexport->name)
                                   (:c-name . vl-dpiexport->c-name)
                                   (:spec . vl-dpiexport->spec)
                                   (:fntask . vl-dpiexport->fntask)
                                   (:atts . vl-dpiexport->atts)
                                   (:loc . vl-dpiexport->loc))
                                 'change-vl-dpiexport
                                 'remake-vl-dpiexport))
