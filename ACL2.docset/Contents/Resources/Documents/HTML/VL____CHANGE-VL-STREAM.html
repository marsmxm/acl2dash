Modifying constructor for vl-stream structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-stream x 
                  [:dir <dir>] 
                  [:size <size>] 
                  [:parts <parts>] 
                  [:atts <atts>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-stream.

We construct a new vl-stream structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-stream

(defmacro change-vl-stream (x &rest args)
          (std::change-aggregate 'vl-stream
                                 x args
                                 '((:dir . vl-stream->dir)
                                   (:size . vl-stream->size)
                                   (:parts . vl-stream->parts)
                                   (:atts . vl-stream->atts))
                                 'change-vl-stream
                                 'remake-vl-stream))
