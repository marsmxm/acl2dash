Click for Atc-uaconvert-values-rules in the Full Manual

Rules about uaconvert-values on values of given types.

These are not used during the symbolic execution; they are used to prove rules used during the symbolic execution.

Definitions and Theorems

Theorem: uaconvert-values-when-scharp-and-scharp

(defthm uaconvert-values-when-scharp-and-scharp
        (implies (and (scharp x) (scharp y))
                 (equal (uaconvert-values x y)
                        (mv (sint-from-schar x)
                            (sint-from-schar y)))))
