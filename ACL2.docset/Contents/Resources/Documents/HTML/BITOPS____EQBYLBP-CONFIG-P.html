(eqbylbp-config-p x) is a defaggregate of the following fields.

  • restriction — Invariant (pseudo-termp restriction).
  • witness-rule — Invariant (acl2::wcp-witness-rule-p witness-rule).
  • instance-rule — Invariant (and (acl2::wcp-instance-rule-p instance-rule) (equal (len (acl2::wcp-instance-rule->vars instance-rule)) 1)) .
  • prune-examples
  • passes — Invariant (posp passes).
  • simp-hint
  • add-hints
  • verbosep
