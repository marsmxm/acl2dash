(vl-modulelist->modinsts x) applies vl-module->modinsts to every member of the list x, and appends together all the resulting lists.

This is an ordinary defmapappend.

Function: vl-modulelist->modinsts

(defun vl-modulelist->modinsts (x)
       (declare (xargs :guard (vl-modulelist-p x)))
       (mbe :logic (if (consp x)
                       (append (vl-module->modinsts (car x))
                               (vl-modulelist->modinsts (cdr x)))
                       nil)
            :exec (reverse (vl-modulelist->modinsts-exec x nil))))
