Retrieve the untranslated body of a function.

Signature
(ibody fn wrld) → body
Arguments
fn — Guard (symbolp fn).
wrld — Guard (plist-worldp wrld).
Returns
body — An untranslated term.

This is as introduced (hence the i in the name) by the user.

Function: ibody

(defun
     ibody (fn wrld)
     (declare (xargs :guard (and (symbolp fn) (plist-worldp wrld))))
     (let ((__function__ 'ibody))
          (declare (ignorable __function__))
          (car (last (get-defun-event fn wrld)))))
