Modifying constructor for vl-genblock structures.

Syntax
(change-vl-genblock x 
                    [:name <name>] 
                    [:elems <elems>] 
                    [:condnestp <condnestp>] 
                    [:loc <loc>])

This is an often useful alternative to make-vl-genblock.

We construct a new vl-genblock structure that is a copy of x, except that you can explicitly change some particular fields. Any fields you don't mention just keep their values from x.

Definition

This is an ordinary change- macro introduced by defprod.

Macro: change-vl-genblock

(defmacro
     change-vl-genblock (x &rest args)
     (std::change-aggregate 'vl-genblock
                            x args
                            '((:name . vl-genblock->name)
                              (:elems . vl-genblock->elems)
                              (:condnestp . vl-genblock->condnestp)
                              (:loc . vl-genblock->loc))
                            'change-vl-genblock
                            'remake-vl-genblock))
